Sundary morning.

Dearestest – I am writing this while lazing deliciously in bed as I am quite tired out by my yesterday’s performance.

[p. 4]

I’ve had a grand “banquet” luncheon at the Ritz which was so crowded that the lounging room, foyer & ball, were all filled up with tables. Everybody was awfully well dressed & gay and excited and the band was something gorgeous! so exhilirating – after luncheon we motored down to the polo field in Eugene’s car – I say motored – but in reality we crept – for [ double underlined: never] anywhere, have I seen so many cars.
