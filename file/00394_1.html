Wednesday Oct. 27

Dearest B.B.–

I have just this morning received your letter of Oct. 7th which has encouraged a little tiny bit as it you did not definitely decide not to come over – To put it up to me is an awfully difficult thing because I am so purely selfish about it – I want you to come

[p. 2]

over terribly – more so than I could ever say – of course you will have a perfectly splendid time as you always do, once you are here – and I shall be happy for a [sic] least three months after you are gone and ( truly ) in Heaven while you are here.
