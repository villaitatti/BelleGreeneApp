I went to a luncheon today in a grand mansion on upper Fifth Avenue which a quasi-friend of mine has just built. It was lots of fun because we were a perfectly irresponsible crowd – just we two women & Percy Mackaye – Poultney Bigelow, Rann Kennedy & Oliver Herford & the [^only^] reason I have evenmentioned it is to shock you by repeating Oliver’s latest bon mot. After luncheon, Mrs. Van Rensselaer took us over the new house, very grand & imposing & very

[p. 12]

tiresome – In passing through her personal suite we came upon a large & elaborate bath room whose only ornament was a huge douche-bag hung on the wall – Oliver Herford & I, were in the rear, making nasty catty remarks about the house but when Oliver spied this bag, he raised his hands piously & said to me in a hoarse & overloud whisper “lest we beget” –

Are you shocked? I howled!
