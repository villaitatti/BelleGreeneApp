Wednesday, 26 Dec.

1923

Dear B.B. –

I really did hope to get a Christmas line to you or failing that a word for the New Year, but I never seem to have a free or quiet moment – My unanswered correspondence hangs like the old man of the sea about my shoulders and has reached a point at which excuses are futile –

I am glad you liked our MS. which Cockerell & Co. are to publish for the Roxburghe
