Thursday, February 24

My dear – my most beloved of men –

This will doubtless be a shocking scrawl as I am writing propped up on a couch – utterly exhausted and yet I cannot & will not allow another minute to pass without sending you my love –

I succeeded in getting the Big Chief off last Wednesday and collapsed utterly that

[p. 2]

same night – I came down here on Saturday and return tomorrow. I am stopping with Mrs. John Carter Brown,whom perhaps you know, in one of the quaintest and most interesting houses I have ever seen – It is almost 200 years old & is filled with the most beautiful Chippendale & Sheraton furniture, Lowestoft China, old silver & plate to delight the heart of a collector.
