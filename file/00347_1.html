August 24th

[p. 1]

Dearest B.B –

I did not write you on Saturday and Sunday, [^ both ^] because I was busyevery minute of Saturday and went up to Belle Haven (Conn.) for the week-end at 6 o’clock and was so exhausted that it was all I could do to sit through a long dinner party the Favershams gave us (I was visiting the GutzonBorglum's – He’s an American sculptor of no very

[p. 2]

great artistic value, but having a wonderful and fascinating personality – and I love him very much) I motored back immediately after the dinner, at ten o’clock, went to bed and stayed there until noon – We had a number of people (all men) for luncheon among whom was the Great Theodore Roosevelt – I was highly excited because they placed me next to him!
