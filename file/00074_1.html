[p. 1]

Monday, July 25

You most unsatisfactory man. This is to be purely a scolding letter – In the first place you have not written to me for aeons – not since July 9 or 10 – for which you ought to be punished

[p. 2]

In the Second place what in Thunder do you want to be in Munich in August for? – I cannot possibly go there. I leave here on the 10th & go straight to London where I must stay at least one week
