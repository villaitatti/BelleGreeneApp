Sunday 21

life is horribly dull here – at least I feel dull – I had lots of invitations to go out of town this week end but did not feel in the mood and have been here (at the Library) all day – and have accomplished much that I set out to – mostly writing business letters which I hate. The way in which they accumulate is nothing short of monstrous. Almost everyone I know is abroad and I am positively aching

[p. 4]

for Paris – Agoness sends nothing but pastels so I return the correspondence in the same way – By the time you get this, I dare say she will be in London – I am enclosing an article about Johnson which may (or may not) interest you. I feel frightfully dull – So will go home to read Laufer’s book – I feel horribly lonely and blue. I suppose it’s my own fault. Wish you were here or I were there Ever Your Belle
