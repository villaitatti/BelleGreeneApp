Tuesday – June 15 –

Beloved that I want you to be –

I am going to scold you – no – not scold you but beg of you on my knees – if you have any spark of friendship – affection or love for me – please don’t jeer at my dearly beloved Boss –

You can’t know how your perhaps thoughtless words hurt me – they cut like a whip and make me physically – mentally and at heart sick –
