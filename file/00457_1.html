July 20, 1920

Dear B.B. –

Your letters from Paris have been most welcome & interesting – I have not written oftener because I have been "shut away" from people with a swollen right jaw – I thought I had got rid of all that when I paid $500. – to t remove an unerupted wisdom tooth from my system, but it seems that either before or during the removal some limphatic (??) nerve [^ gland ^] was affected – so that now I spend my idle hours sipping asperin [sic] & pressing an ice pack to me face – It all makes me blue & irritable so that I keep doubly busy here to "divart me attention" – I wonder what caused the burst of affection in yours of the 18 from Paris, received today – I imagine nothing more than a wave of remembrance.
