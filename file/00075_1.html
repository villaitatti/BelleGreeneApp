Tuesday July 26

Insatiable man that you are how do you like a daily letter from me? –

This is in answer to your cable of yesterday – It is quite settled that I am to sail on the

[p. 2]

Oceanic on August 10 – I will go direct to London where I do not want to stay more than a week – I have not taken rooms yet but want to find some quiet place & for this reason may go to Fleming’s Hotel
