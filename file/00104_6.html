My dear this is a [ quadruple underlined : disgusting] letter & I’m going to close it – I am all out of sorts – tired to death – sick of every thing and want only you truly dear –

‘ a toi .’ I am sending you my only decent possession for your Christmas, it is

a little Syrakuse [sic] coin c. 400 B.C. in the style of Kimon – I gave it to Canessa to mount for me and he made a perfectly disgusting job of it but as I must send it by the Lusitan Mauretania so as to reach you I hope by Christmas – Canessa was horrified to think I would give it away
