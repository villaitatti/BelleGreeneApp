Tuesday Jan. 24 – 8:30 p.m.

[ circled, in secretarial script : Our [ printed letterhead ] Apartment!]

You seedaarrlingI am already beginning a letter to you although I mailed one only an hour ago – This is the first written from “our” apartment. It is not quite in order but I have a desk and a chair and heaps of paper & a pen & that is all I need to talk to you –

I have just finished reading, for the second time today,

[p. 2]

Your truly adorable letter of the 10th – in answer to four of mine which you received at the same time – You have no idea what joy it gives me to know that my letters mean so much to you – I only wish I had the pen ofmenand angels that I might write better andoftener.
