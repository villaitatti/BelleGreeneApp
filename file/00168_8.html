It must be wonderful in Paris now – I really envy you – I will cable you tomorrow, because I like to feel you so near – and also to let you know at once that I cannot make any definite plans.

A great many of my friends are beginning

[p. 16]

returning from Europe and I dread having them back as they do not give me a moment’s peace – Ethel Grant sailed on the 8th or 9th and the Brunner’s are coming on the 18 – I shall be glad to see them because they can give me news of you – I am going up to my little country place on Sunday morning
