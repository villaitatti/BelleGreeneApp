Monday June 7 –

Princeton, N.J.

Combien de temps, dis, la belle

Dis, veux-tu m’être fidèle?

Pour une nuit, pour un jour

Mon amour –

L’amour nous flatte et nous touche

Du doigt, de l’oeil, de la bouche

Pour un jour, pour une nuit

Et s’enfuit –

I thought I knew my

Swinburne pretty well but I have just discovered the above which I love – Perhaps you know it but I never came across it before –

Beloved – See how fidèle I am – for I am writing this in bed – before they bring up my breakfast – as I am sure I will not find another minute – I am having an awfully good time – being “rushed” to
