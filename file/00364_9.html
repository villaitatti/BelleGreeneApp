As I have heard this from several different sources, I daresay there may be some truth in it –

Of course, like everyone else, I daily receive letters asking me to raise money for all sorts of reasons. the last (big one) is from Lady Johnstone who calmly asks me to raise $25000 – for a private ambulance which she wants to take out on her own –

I find that most of all this relief work is a specie of self-advertising
