Saturday, May 30 –

Darlingest – This is our great Decoration Day – really a messy Labor day – when the masses pic k nic in the park, the streets and in fact all over the City. I have had most of my weekend plans broken up by my two male infants so am going up to Eugene Meyer’s Farm to join them all this afternoon. Much as I love Read, I shall be a wee bit glad when he goes back as I have not a free minute

[p. 2]

to myself – Later midnight

Dearest B.B. (myy angel)

I brought this beginning of a letter to the Farm with me and I am writing this in bed, when I am supposed to be asleep. I got up here at 6:30 and found Eugene, Read & Raphael, hilariously gay from some bare-footed and bare-faced dancing they had witnessed in the Greek theatre at Mt. Kisco – I proceeded to sober them up – We went for a little walk and then had an A. no. 1 dinner. After dinner we went out in Eugene’s new motor
