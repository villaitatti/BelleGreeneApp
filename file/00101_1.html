Friday p.m. –

Fiamma mia –

This morning I received your letter of Nov. 4 – the last I dare say you will have written from Paris – It pleased and distressed me both – pleased me because you told me you loved me a great deal and distressed me doubly – first because I had hurt & worried you in connection with the
