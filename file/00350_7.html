What I propose to do is to get a group of women together of no set – but one from each set – that is all the Club Presidents, all the Educational heads, charity heads etc. etc. & form them into a committee & have each one raise a fund in her particular club or school or whatever it is

I shall write to Mme. Vanderwelde & write her to meet these women at luncheon and I will trust to her to enthuse them –

Tuesday Sept. 15
