Tuesday March 1 –

Dear Man of my dreams –

How glad I was to have your letter of the 16th from Settignano and how sad to know that you were suffering with a cold – That house really must be exasperating. I shall shriek with joy on the day you write me it is completely finished and you are at last comfortably settled at home. Thank you so much

[p. 2]

for the Reinach Elucidation of the Leonardo-Lucas matter. As yet I have only read the first page, just having received it this morning – but I shall plunge into it at the first opportunity. I have already read Bode’s pamphlet.
