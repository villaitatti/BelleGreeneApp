first part of our Catalogue of Babylonian & other Cuneiform inscriptions

These are now off my hands and will be ready for distribution on J. P.’s return; and I have clumped back with relief – I am expecting J. P. on the 18th of this month and have a great many things to get in order for him. I find my library much too small for the number of books, so I have before me the not-unpleasant job of weeding out.
