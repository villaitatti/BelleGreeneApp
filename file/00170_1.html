Wednesday Oct. 11

Dear B. B. –

I am filled with a certain kind of envy of the wonderful place you are visiting – Not only the places themselves, but your description of them is so very attractive

[p. 2]

that I feel compelled to put on my bonnet and rush off to the first steamer – As it is, I can make no headway in my plans for Europe and it almost looks now as if I would not get over at all .
