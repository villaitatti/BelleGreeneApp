Samedi soir

Dear beloved Fiama mia –

I have just received your wire saying you will be here about five on Monday and you know what a leap of joy my heart gave. I had so thoroughly prepared myself for all sorts of heart aches and sacrifices that I find

it really almost a shock to have you coming back to me once more – of course you know how I must feel to have urged you to take that [double underlined: beastly] journey quite uselessly I assure you I awoke at night scourging myself for having at all interfered in the matter!
