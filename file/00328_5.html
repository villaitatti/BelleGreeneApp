Thursday, June 18th

9:30 a.m.

Darlingest –

What a wonderful surprise to find a letter from you this morning, yours of June 7th–8th. The

[p. 10]

ever to be praised Olympia got it here so quickly. In view of this last letter everything I wrote yesterday is “out seasoned” but I shall let it go just the same. You make me feel very unhappy in the way you speak of Read – mind you, not you personally – but I mean in the things you “quote” as being currently said about him.
