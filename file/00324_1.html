Thursday June 4

Dearest B. B. –

Behold me, devoted as ever to our friend, Sir Hercules, but tickled to death that he has departed – you know how little used I am to being at anyone’s beck and call and being lowed and hampered by the obligation of anyone’s pressure and much as I liked Read, I

[p. 2]

was really fretting terribly under the continued strain.
