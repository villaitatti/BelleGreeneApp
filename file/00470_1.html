Sunday morning – 13 March

Dear B.B.

Behold my large mindedness – In spite of no word from you I continue busily to write.

Yesterday I walked 8 miles – 3 in the morning and 5 in the afternoon – Slept from 9:30 last night until 7 this morning – have just had a bath in spring water and a glorified breakfast –

This is the "closed" season here so there are only half a dozen people in the place I really did not anticipate that many as they made such loud talk about "inviting" me at this time – when the house was practically closed for spring cleaning

There are four men and two women guests besides myself – One of the men interests me (temporarily) a good deal. He's rather an extraordinary creature, the product of an English mother and a Japanese father – educated at Eton and Oxford – I have not yet succeeded in pinning down his name, wanted to ask him to spell or write it for me but did not dare.
