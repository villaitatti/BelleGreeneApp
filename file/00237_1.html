May 19, 1913

Dear B. B.

I have just received your letter of May 7th – a very much “puzzled” letter – I suppose you really cannot understand me – but if you could you would not like (or love!) me. Now would you B.B.?

I am

[p. 2]

wonderfully cheered today by a letter I have received from Junius Morgan in Paris – in which he says the grandest things to me and about me – I would rather have him say all these things than anyone else for it was he who first suggested me for the place –
