Xmas

Daarrling– It is Christmas Day and alas! you are not here and I only know it is this festive day thanks to the calendar & various lovely and unlovely gifts; chief of which is your little SanGiminiuslegend –

[p. 2]

Dear I do so love to receiveany thingfrom you. I don’t care what it is although you always send me enchanting things which reminds me that I forget to tell you how much I enjoyed Lord Dunsany’s “Dreamer’s tales” I liked especially the Madness of Andelsprutz & Ebb & Flow – although I have not read them all yet.
