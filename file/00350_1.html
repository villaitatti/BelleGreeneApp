Thursday Sept. 10

Darling B.B. –

There is little in my mind and heart except you and war – and there is little or nothing else to write you about – Junius Morgan arrived from Paris yesterday and I spent hours talking to him. He is keenly enthusiastic about the courage and staunch patriotism of the French people and related innumerable instances of families whose

men with the exception of sons under 16 years of age had gone to the war – He said that up to a week before he left Paris, the chief hardship had been lack of money – He told me how he had happened to go to the bank (Morgan Harjes) unsuspectingly to go cash a note for a friend for something like $250.00 – the bank official was so surly and obstinate about it that Junius got mad and drew out all of his money – something like $20,000.00 Of course, being
