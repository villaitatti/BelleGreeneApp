I’m pretty sure the very nice woman who came to see me today does not in the least suspect what I am going to do – the other was from Vassar College and I refused but promptly suggested [ double underlined : Thursty] – I went out & worked over her for twenty minutes and was quite paralyzed with surprise when she consented! So I dragged her in & found that she & this woman were old college friends – so she agreed to make her be in public & will talk on “the value of the private collector”

I am going to give her a paper I read at the Bryn

Mawr Club and she will add or subtract & come out all right – It’s amusing. She is so excited about it – as Vassar is her own college (‘86!!!) Among the dealers, was Joe Duveen who seems to think he was the Morgan collection cinched – I listened to everything he had to say & egged him on to say more and told him I would help him as much as I could. He sailed tonight on the Mauretania. I also arranged an interview between him and Jack Morgan which came off quite happily.
