Tuesday May 14

Dear B. B. –

Thanks so much for the p.c. showing the Campanile which you sent me – It was really interesting and it must have been glorious to have been there at the dedication. J. P. seems to have enjoyed it

[p. 2]

immensely and from your letters and the newspaper clippings he sent me I got a fine idea of the occasion –
