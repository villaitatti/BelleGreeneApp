Tuesday May 14

Dear B. B. –

Thanks so much for thep.c.showing the Campanile which you sent me – It was really interesting and it must have been glorious to have been there at the dedication. J. P. seems to have enjoyed it

immensely and from your letters and the newspaper clippings he sent me I got a fine idea of the occasion –
