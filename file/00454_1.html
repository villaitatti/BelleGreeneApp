[ In a lighter ink : Tuesday June 10, 1920]

[p. 1]

Dear B.B.

I interviewed the people at the Ritz for you today & absolutely nothing is to be had in November, December or January –

The place the McClellan’s spoke of is out of the question as the apartments there are rented by the year – Tomorrow Iwillgo to the Hotel Chatham which is around the corner from Carl Hamilton's
