Friday March 8

Dear B. B. –

I was so worried to receive Mary’s letter telling me of your accident but so relieved as I read on to find that you were improving rapidly – It must have been a very painful and nasty business and it distressed me

[p. 2]

very much. It was very sweet of Mary to write me – I cannot write to thank her today but will do so at the first opportunity

I am feeling better every day – am going through a treatment of intestinal massage which I think is going to help me greatly – Also my eyes are improving rapidly
