Summer – Simply to get the family out of town – but believe me it won’t see very much of little Belle – It is far to commute every day & as I have week-end invitations for almost the entire summer I do not see when I can get there.

It’s one of those “cebbrity” colonies – Richard le Gallienne, David Bispham & that crowd – I’ve known them too well to be still interested in them – but the “family” idolize them all so they, at least, will have a good time.

There is absolutely nothing to write you about except the appalling heat – You really cannot imagine how dreadful it is here –
