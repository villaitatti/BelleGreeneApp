Dearest – I was certainly asleep – As I wrote the above as it is a Fra Angelico, which has been bought by Boston – Certainly I am growing old and nutty. I see by this mornings [sic] paper that Grant Richards has finally divorced his wife. She must be very happy. I do not know her at all, but have heard much of her from Kennerley. I also hear that Grant Richards is irretrievably in love with Mrs. Sterner, so perhaps that is why he finally gave in –

Now I will send this off dear – surely you must be exhausted by these long letters
