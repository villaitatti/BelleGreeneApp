Saturday, March 14

9 a.m.

Dearest B.B. –

Your adorable letter of yesterday has just reached me – and much as I want to get at “loving” you right here and now, I must scold you first for not detailing to me all you [sic] sassiety doings – Every bit of it and all the nice and flattering things that

[p. 2]

are said of you and done for you – So go and lie no more B. B dear –

I do not dare write too long as I am anxious to have this reach you tonight. I would have written last night after Agoness’ dinner but I was simply bored speechless and could hardly totter into bed when I finally reached my apartment.
