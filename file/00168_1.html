Friday Sept. 8

Dear B. B. –

My butyou are the giddy one. – For a frail and delicate person you can put in morefestivties[sic] than any other such invalid I ever heard of –

I am so glad to know that you feel so well and that your stay at St. Moritz is benefitting you so greatly. I am

[p. 2]

as always, much interested in your doings and am so glad that more people adore you for yourself each year –
