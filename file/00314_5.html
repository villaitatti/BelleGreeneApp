Wednesday, April 29

Nothing of interest today darling. I lunched with a pack of “Feminists” at the Colony Club and sat dumb most of the time – talked “art” when I was supposed to – and left a quieter & sadder female –

Tuesday April 30

What do you think dearest? I went to bed and to sleep at ten o’clock last night consequently feel utterly worn out this morning. Had dinner in the grill of the Biltmore with Arnold Genthe

[p. 8]

who spoiled it all by insisting upon my going to inspect Mrs. Guinness’ portrait of him before – he said she had begged him to have me look at it – If he tells her what I said about it, she’ll never want me to look at another effort of hers – It’s the dirtiest mess I ever saw –

In the foreign mail yesterday, I received the two Foucher books on Buddhist iconography. They look formidable but I guarantee to read em – [double underlined: with joy]! Thank you so much dearest – I have written to the British Museum to send you the Ku Kai Chi [sic] reproduction
