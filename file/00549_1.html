Tuesday 5 Oct. 1926

[ In the top margin: Am sending your proof sheets–the 3tiragesetc. to I Tatti by this same mail ]

Well, B.B. dear, this is my 4th day at home – We got in early Saturday morn the appalling hurricane-tempesta having delayed us and prevented the record they hoped to make, but I have a hunch that had I been on any other boat, one B.G. would be in Davy Jones locker now, so I am correspondingly grateful.

I have done practically nothing since my return but read a mountainous lot of correspondence, presumably

[p. 2]

‘personal’ and so left for me to tackle. Fortunately I am in a state of health and huskiness which allows me to eat it up without any attendant weariness – Heaps of gossip of all kinds from all over the world – Heavens what a lot is “doing”! I have put aside endless choice bits, which I’ll compile for your delectation as soon as things slow down a bit – Commencing on the 11th of this month, I am going down to Princeton 1 or 2 days a week for Gabriel Millet’s lectures on Byzantine MSS. and when he is through there & before he goes to Harvard
