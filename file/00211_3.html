I am waiting for Prof. Hyvernat to return from Rome to find out if and when I must go there. It will probably now be (if at all) in the horrid month of November although it would be nice to have a birthday with you – nothing new has

[p. 5]

developed – I am getting a bit tired again but am going to keep myself well and strong by massage and Swedish gymnastics in the morning – There is a fine young woman here who has just come from some very successful work at Aix-les-bains and she is coming to me every morning at seven. Gee! but I’ll hate to get up.
