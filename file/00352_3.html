He claims that it was really a white elephant on Duveen’s hands, as they had bought it with Altman in view, & after him, Mr. Morgan & with them both out of the field, they were very glad to sell it to Widener on any terms. He also said that Widener had given some things in exchange as part payment. He has had me in screams of laughter over his two families – the English and the Italian families as he calls them. From what he says, I infer, that he did not
