and half an hour wake up to find I have been dreaming and have not written a line – I have been quite wretched myself – just plain tired & a little bit discouraged – Not by any definite thing You may rejoice to know that I have given up all of my hoodlum friends, except Ethel & her I see very rarely – She told me she wrote you a horrid letter about me – I am rushing this with heaps of love –

Yours Ever Belle.
