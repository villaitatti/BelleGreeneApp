May 16, 1911

Daarrling –

Although I wrote you yesterday – I must write you again today as I have just been plunged in a fathomless sea of reminiscences by your cable from Siena

To think that you are there and without me – Do you remember the evening we sat out on the walls and looked back at the city silhouetted against the wonderful pink sky? I shall never forget those moments

I remember that there was a tiny bit of sadness mixed in our joy and that I wondered at the time
