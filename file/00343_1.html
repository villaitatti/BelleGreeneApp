Tuesday Aug. 4th

B.B. dearest –

I have just learned that the Lusitania by which I intended to send this will not sail and that the only safe boat is the St. Paul on Friday –

We are all alarmed here about friends who are stranded in various parts of Europe – One of my friends, Mrs. Carman Roberts just landed last night after eleven days on the water. They

[p. 2]

sailed from Havre and after two days out turned around & were returning to Havre when a French cruiser came to their aid & escorted them most of the way here. Mary said they were not allowed to send any wirelesses but were actually bombarded by them from German cruisers – I am worried about little Mrs. Flowerdale.
