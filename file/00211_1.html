Tuesday Oct. 1, 1912

Belovedest –

I am at last installed at the above address and not crazy about it except that I am alone and quiet & have lots of sunshine and fresh air – quite a number of almost unattainable things in New York –

But – alas – nothing fits and nothing looks well –

By the way I am sorry you did not answer (as yet) my letter from Boston about the B. A. as I fear it will be too late if I do not hear in a day or two – In any case keep what I wrote you confidential . I
