31 Dec. 1920 –

Dear B.B. –

All hail & wishes for 1921 – Hope it will prove more "heureuse"than 1920.

As my cold hangs on distressingly & everyone prophesies that I shall shortly pass out wrapped in the arms of pneumonia, I'm not a very cheerful person and except when I lose all thought of myself in work – do not feel up to much – I have dined out (or gone out in the evenings)
