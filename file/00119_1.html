Friday 6:00 p.m. –

Daarrling – I received the itinery [sic] today by the Lusitania – I only had a moment in which to look at the first sheet & the last & it almost broke my heart – really upset me for the day – I don’t seem to find a moment in which to write you – I’m completely worn

out & look like the wrath of God – horrid black circles under my eyes – I am so tired – Ethel Harrison is a human leech she won’t let me alone a minute – will make no engagements with anyone except me – as J. P. is still here I can’t give her any time outside in the daytime so she spends all her time sitting beside
