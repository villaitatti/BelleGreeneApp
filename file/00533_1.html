26 May, 1926.

B.B. you old Darlint

Your letter from Girgenti (is that a place or a Disease?) amused me hugely – I snorted with rage and chortled with glee – Instead of the likes of you being glad that I keep the corps(e) of me pure and chaste, here you are reviling me for not allowing it to be a masculine urinal!

Sure I’m pure in heart body & mind – which reminds me, I only realized about a year ago, why your damned old sex gets all

[p. 2]

het-up. & I’m sure its because I throw it all off, sorter sweat it out – in seeing things I like – in interest in the job & all sorts of things & people connected with it – and in a kind of real love and affection for the few people I love (you being pretty near the top) and that kind is too deep to be ‘sexey’ – for after all, that’s a crust very near the surface isn’t it now really?

So that’s that.

I’ve never been to Sicily. I’m acheing [sic] to go there. Perhaps I can do it next year. Is Mrs. Royall Tyler
