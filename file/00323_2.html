We motored for three hours and it certainly was enchanting when we came back & sat around an open fire as it was a bit chilly and – here I am in bed – I can’t help but think every minute of the week end we spent here – and how different it was – how charged and surcharged the atmosphere was and how I nestled in your arms in the afternoon when the others went out – I miss you dearest – I long for you and I want you – very, very, much. B.

Sunday night and again Midnight

This seems to be “our” hour darlingest – I have had such a busy out door day and have just reached home – We all four walked all morning and in the afternoon, after lunching, Eugene & I raced our cars! I took Read in mine and he was frightened breathless . [ double underlined: Needless] to say we won by three minutes.
