Tuesday March 8th

Most dearly loved –

I hope you notice how I am warming up to the correspondence game! although this I fear will be but a scrappy note simply because I hate to have the Lusitania go out without bearing you a bit of my love – I am really a

[p. 2]

physical wreck, which seems queer, because I never knew that I had a back bone up to a year ago – to add to my weariness I have a “woman’s” luncheon at one o’clock – a tedious & boring affair which I could not refuse as my dear Mrs. Hamilton is giving it – afterwards we are going up to the Plaza to hear Edith Wynne Matthison
