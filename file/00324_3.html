Friday June 5

Darlingest B. B – Such a heavenly letter from you today. I was awfully amused at Ms. Wharton’s quasi-jealous outbreak & can sympathize with her perfectly. It is doubtless just the way I should feel were I to meet all your

[p. 6]

Beloved’s at one board – I have received the Grünwedel’s Altbuddhistische Kunst which you sent me – It looks terrifically learned and you know how frail my german is – but I shall do my best and in any case the illustrations interest me immensely.
