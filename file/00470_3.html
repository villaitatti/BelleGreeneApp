I won't have anything to do with the other humans here – two complacently rich factory owners from Pennsylvania and the Preston Gibsons. I have always considered her, in spite of her former social height, one of the lowest of New York's products – She is of a disgusti canaillerie –

This is an endlessly enchanting place – I can hardly realize that we are only 5 hours from New York – the morning Times and Herald which arrive with one's breakfast tray and the finished service and comfort are the only traces of "city life"

I adore my idleness here and am seriously considering giving up my Palm Beach trip, for more of it

Freer was here a good deal and they declare that he was engaged to Miss Rhodes (who generally accompanied him) and that the marriage was put off, several times only because of his ill health – old John D. has scribbled his name on the newel post along with countless other so called celebrities (?) – In spite of their "exclusive" talk, it strikes me that their preference is for notorieties – I'm sure they'd prefer a lion-tameress to Miss Hewitt for instance – I assured them laughingly that I had grown very exclusive in my old age and refused to sign – so did the nice Jap – But
