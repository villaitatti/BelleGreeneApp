July 28, 1913

Dear B. B. –

I haveonlyjust today received your letter of the 19th from London – surely I prefer the station mails! You are still more tired – I cannot quite understand it or forgive you for it – You blessed child, why don’t you stay home at nights and rest , instead of your Russian ballets – and above all – yourtheatreparties – Howyoudid scold me when I did it – and I had all sorts of vitalitybackof me which you have not! But I know what it is to be scolded – even from afar so Iwillnot give you any more of
