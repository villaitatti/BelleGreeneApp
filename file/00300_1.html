Friday, April 3

10 p.m.

Dear hugable [sic] B.B. –

You will smile one of your “Chesire Cat” affects I am sure, when I tell you that not for a single second have you been out of my thoughts or life today – I can hear you say now “I told her so but she was so blankety stupid she would not listen” –

I went to the Museum betimesthis morning,

hoping and praying to have at least my first look alone and I was lucky in having a whole quiet hour to myself – and it really was delightful.
