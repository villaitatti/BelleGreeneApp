Later –

Jack M. came in this morning and bored me to death for almost an hour talking about Read, whom he seems quite dippy about. He asked me to spend Friday night at Glen Cove with them, but I had already arranged a dinner party for Read & Murray & so begged him not to snatch them from me, as of course, they would have had to go if he wanted them as they are "hirelings" like me.

I had a long letter from Read in today's mail commanding me to be nice to Murray and I will as much as I can, but I don't find the idea of him, very attractive. Have not seen or heard from Agoness for at least two days!

The wonderful statue is before me as I write. It is so beautiful, So simple and direct, so enveloped in its own calm – that I find myself living in it. I
