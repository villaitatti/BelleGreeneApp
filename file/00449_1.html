Monday, Apr. 19

Dear B.B –

Permit me, in the name of All the Holy Fathers (the only people I know here!) to welcome you to the Holy City – which I adore.

I'm sorry that I am tied up at the Vatican tomorrow (Tuesday) morning with Gasquet and Mercati and they seem

to be so darned important that I don't dare change it. You see I had expected to spend Monday morning with you and so had made my professional duties wait over until Tuesday –

Therefore please sleep all tomorrow morning as you will be exhausted from your journey and come here to lunch with me at 1:15 – I have kept the afternoon free for "us"
