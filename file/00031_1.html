Monday July 12

Beloved dear – This is really only a line (great relief to you I am sure) to [illegible deletion] enclose the P.O. reply in re the photograph. I also enclose your registry receipt. They have evidently been sent back to Italy & I presume are at the P.O. there awaiting direction from you. Do send them to

[p. 3]

[Sheet rotated ninety degrees to right for this page]

Washington & from there I learn they were opened & returned to the address within – Therefore , I presume one went back to Paris & the others to I Tatti – I have been sailing all day & dancing all evening and am too tired (not to say sunburned & warm!) to write more. Did you go to the Reid ball? It must have been very fine – I am so over joyed that my dear Boss is returning!! As for you I could shake you & kiss you in the same moment –

Devotedly

Belle .
