[written on diagonal in top left corner: Received today your letter of the 17 via the K.K.K. Wilhelm ]

Tuesday August 24

This is a desperate effort to catch the Mauritania. I received the photographs of you (from Florence) yesterday. I love the actual photograph. The painting is of some man I might have loved had I not met you first, and I am awfully glad I did not know you “aetas 21”!

Mr. Morgan returned to us (I nearly said to me !) this morning looking superbly well & in splendid spirits from his cruise.

Now that Harriman has returned – and not in a coffin –

the American public is permitting itself to draw a long breath. The way interest has been concentrated on his health and return from health-seeking is, I presume characteristic of the day, but utterly repulsive and disgusting to me. The poor foolish ones have wrecked the market and thereby ruined only themselves with their useless “Theories” guesses & fears as to what Harriman will do with the Pacifics’ finances in the existing posture of affairs

To me, the only interest converges on the question of how this quintessence of activity, this cunning yet gigantic schemer, will adapt himself to the remorseless programme of rest,
