Have a German lesson tonight & my Chinese lesson all tomorrow – a dinner in the evening with the Baruch’s which will be a contrast to last night’s in that every one there (mostly men) will be worth while. I forgot to tell you that Eugene had to leave at 9:15 last evening to speak at a

Yale dinner so I could not talk to him – I will surely do so before you return and hope I can arrange things for you –

Did you meet McAdoo in Washington? To think he should marry that Wilson girl – Many “lady” hearts are bleeding here, mine would bleed in unison were it not that he threw me over several
