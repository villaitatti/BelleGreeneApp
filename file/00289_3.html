Jones family – Beatrice came in to see me the other day, looking more like a homesick camel than ever and told me about her life at New Haven which she enjoys immensely – She seems to be enjoying married life, but how that charming Farrand man can stand her I cannot see –

After luncheon I went up to the American Museum of Natural History to see the Chinese pottery (Han) described in Laufer’s book – It was very

[p. 5]

good as far as it went, and beautifully displayed – Then I went down to see the Mexican things of which I had caught a glimpse in climbing the endless stairs to the Chinese floor – And there, B.B. I did have a treat and a surprise. Of course everything was merely in cast but they were all a revelation to me – and puzzled me a good bit – they seemed to be, not new, but a combination of many other things to me.
