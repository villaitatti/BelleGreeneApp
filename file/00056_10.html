sweet – Dear I have so much I want to say to you – but I have already written far too much & I have a terrifying feeling that you are bored – Perish the thought!

That I love you, you know always – that I miss you and want you to a sickening extent you also know – but do you know how often I am with you, sitting in your lap as I did in those memorable days and melting into your very being – Do you know that? –

I love you dear – Goodnight –

Belle
