Friday Oct. 13

Dear B.B. –

I can imagine your rage and hatred of me when I confess that this is the first line I have written you for over a week – Thank fortune you are 3000 miles away and cannot beat me . Well

I could give you many excuses – a daily visit to a brute dentist who has callously removed four nerves and consequently made a physical wreck of me – something or other I had to go to every night which did me up the next day and a new job I’m engineering (without any financial return to me )
