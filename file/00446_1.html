Mar. 19. 1920

Dearest B.B. –

Just received yours of the 15th – the enclosed is the sort of thing I never answer nor pay any attention to. We have, I fear, become hardened to agents or friends ofstaring Russians, with incredible art objects to sell, in America. I daresay your friend Duveen would be interested. No – neither the sharks nor

fleas nor "lady friends" which, do not bother or embitter my young life at all – I am wise to them after the first couple of lines of a letter, whereupon I tear it up and pay no further attention – It would be utter stupidity to allow them to bother me –
