Thursday, 4 p.m.

Dear – I am off in an hour for Boston. I tried to “sneak” some time in which to write you this morning but Mr. Jack came in very early and has been here ever since

His secretary is laid low with a cold so he politely commanded me to help him out and I have been hard at it ever since – I feel awfully blue and depressed
