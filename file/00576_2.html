the smart shops are doing nothing – One night at dinner at the Meurice, there were only 2 other groups of diners!

Here on the contrary, everything is bustling – Much to my surprise I found this Hotel full – at this time of the year!

I am so glad to be here – the people, even down to the bus boys are such a sharp contrast in their courtesy & self-respect – to Paris.

By the way will you tell Nicky that her nice man did not come to see me, and as I did not know where he was stopping in N.Y. I could not get in touch with him.

Next summer I swear to get to my beloved Italy again – Do Write and tell me all your news – I will be in N.Y. again on Sept. 22 – Love to you all –

Belle
