July 11, 1910 –

Dear & sweet Beloved

I have just this moment received yours of July 3 from Paris – and as always after a letter from you I feel like the “light weight champion” of the world – like Peter Pan – Eternal joy – I always wish

[p. 2]

they were longer & I always wish they came daily instead of fortnightly. There is nothing very new or startling to record here – The heat is suffocating & really hard to bear during the day – I go out of town every day at three & sometimes go off on the yacht with the Big Chef but I don’t care much to do that.
