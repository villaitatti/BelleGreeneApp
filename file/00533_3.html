going to Sinai again with an expedition of half a dozen others. His course in the Old Testament at Harvard is the most popular in the entire University – packed & jammed at every sitting & the boys call it “Smut I” which I think is recommendation for anyone – He’s [double underlined: very] fond of your B.G – & I like him too, but in a most chaste way – mentally as well as physically – I’m sure you will enjoy him. He’s really rather learned although he snorts at my saying “mealodraymer” & ‘Homeric’ and a few other of my pet Oxford lingo –

We bought the MS. of one of the early English Miracle or Mystery plays – & J.P. let me bring

[p. 5]

it home – and I’ve had the most divine time reading it, laughing & shouting at Joseph’s complaints of the shame Mary has brought upon him – and at Noah’s sons complaints of his drunkenness & Noah’s language to wife when she refuses to enter the Ark – then weeping (truly) my eyes out over Abraham & Isaac – It’s an adorable thing & I wish it were mine –

Now I must stop – I’m going up to Newport for over the holiday and as there will probably be a crowd there just for those 3 days, I’ll have to get my rest before I go –

Write soon dear B.B – and be sure to tell me if you plan to come over this year –

Rumour hath it that you do –

BLESS YOU –

Belle
