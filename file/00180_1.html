Tuesday Jan. 9, 1912

Dear dear B. B. –

This is the first time in six months that I have ever had leisure to write a letter. To be sure there are millions of things that I might, could or should be

[p. 2]

doing at this moment, but fortunately there is nothing more important that I have to do than writing you to and J. P. by the Lusitania – and I have dedicated the rest of this afternoon to those two jobs –

I am so glad, you
