Tuesday – Election Day

Nov. 8 – 11 a.m.

Dear B.B. –

I am so distracted by people and things that it is only by locking myself in my room for five minutes that I can find time to write you – All the people I have ever known or met

abroad have descended upon us and I have to refuse all my invitations to entertain them. Lady Algy Lennox has arrived and I expect to see lots of her as she runs around with lots of my friends I am going to take her up to the Museumto night day& she is going to join
