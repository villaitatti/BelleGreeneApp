Dec. 24, 1912

Dear B. B. –

This is a tout-à-fait “bizness” note – Mr. Rey has today formally separated from [^Jacques^] Seligmann who will have to

[p. 2]

pay Rey according to his contract for one year – of course, as I have told you, I am very strong for Rey – although I am keeping quiet about it as I can do more for him
