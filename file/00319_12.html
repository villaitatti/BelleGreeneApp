We are all dining together tonight at the Ritz. – Raphael is giving the dinner and inviting everyone who has entertained him here, among them Agoness, Eugene, Thursty. Goodbye dear. I hope to have a letter tomorrow. I have not sent this before this week as there is no steamer until Saturday – I am – [ written in print script : Your] Belle

Friday, May 22 – 1914

Dearest B.B. –
