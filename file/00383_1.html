Friday, Feb . 26th

Dearest B. B. –

I do not know when the last letter I wrote you now is, or when it will reach you as the boat is reported to have had afire somewhere in mid ocean. There is nothing very new to report – The Duveens are much annoyed because someone (I think undoubtedly Knoedler) [^at once^] gave the newspapers the report that he had bought the pictures for Frick as agent – of course Duveen wanted the glory of
