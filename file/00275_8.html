[p. 12]

Saturday morning

Darling –

I have just received yours enclosing the Freer letter. It is quite stunning and I am sending it to Eugene Meyer for his approval & will tell you what he says tonight – I am shockingly busy this morning as Thursty is laid up with a cold & I have to see every one – So no more dear love until after seeing you tonight – I have filled every minute of the time so as to make it “hurry up” But – I shall go home at six and rest a bit – I feel like Alice’s Rabbit – my heart & my body is “beating” and “bleating” All Yours

Belle
