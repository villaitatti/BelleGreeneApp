Enamels in St. Petersburg I can’t spell the man’s name to save my neck but the Catalogue of it published some years ago is wonderful – and my dear J. P. has all but promised me he will bring it over to America. I am wild to see it – He is leaving on the 11th for Egypt – How I wish you and I were going also –

Daarrling where shall we go this year? If I can come over it will not be

[p. 11]

until about the 1st of August & I can probably only stay a month – that is from about August 8 to Sept. 15 & be back here by the end of September.

What shall we do?

I don’t like the idea of your coming to America just now for several reasons – just because of all of the Customs rows & I fear they would get wind of your coming & try to get you to testify for them
