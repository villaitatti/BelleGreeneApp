[ written along top margin in darker ink : Just off to Paris for funeral of Junius Morgan – return tomorrow]

Monday 21 Aug.

Dear B.B –

It is good of you to want Bobbie and me to come to Consuma – but – the heat – the terrific heat here has laid us low – to

such an extent that I am cancelling the few engagements to visit, I had made, and am going off either to the Dolomites or Villa d’Este –

Venice – except for itself – is not my Venice at all this year – Very few
