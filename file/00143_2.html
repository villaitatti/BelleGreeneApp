I shall write you a real letter for Wednesday’s boat but this will come to you loaded down with love & kisses – kisses for all of your dear self – I wish I could see those “forget-me-not” eyes of yours & see you squint

[p. 4]

up your nose but all of that will I See in August –

I ought to have a letter from you tomorrow –

Through all the Eternities my hand Shall be in yours –

Your Belle
