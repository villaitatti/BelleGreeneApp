I am, but between the dates of Mar . 18th & April 15 – please send care Mrs. Ditson as I wrote you in my last. The Big Chief has just sent me a long cable from Rome in which he says that Seligmann is with him there. He is evidently in fine spirits and having a bully time. I am so glad – truly, as you say, I am “yours forever and a day ” – I have now reached the point of humility where I should thank whatever Gods there be for a day with you

Belle–
