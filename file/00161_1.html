Monday July 17

⸺

B. B. daarrling –

I was so glad to get your cable and from London! – It was maddeningly near – I am quite all right now except for the fact that my diet still consists chiefly of quinine – now

[p. 2]

reduced to 14 grains per diem – I hate the stuff! The Doctor would not allow me to go on the proposed trip to Oklahoma, in fact he was furious that I should even suggest it so I have remained very quietly here, doing nothing except going out to restaurant dinners
