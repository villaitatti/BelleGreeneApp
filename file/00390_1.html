Friday May 28 –

Dearest B. B –

That surely was one horrid letter you sent me – as to the “affairs” which you scent from afar it is purely a myth of your own creation. I have been very busy – very tired out and nervously exhausted and moving to two houses, the country house (a camp

[p. 2]

in Coney Island!) and my new little handful of a town house – the latter I go in on the 1st of October but as any present apartments are to be rented in July I had to clean them out and send my things to storage. You know very well that I have periods when I simply cannot write anyone and as writing is not a profession with me as it is with you, it does not come so easily – Besides
