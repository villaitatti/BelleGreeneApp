Thursday, April 9th.

How did you like my yesterdays epistle darling. I hope it’s [sic] length did not exhaust you! I was “kilt” yesterday with a Hell inspired headache and drooped around all day. Went to luncheon with Dr. Hyvernat and John Ryan – both being good Catholics they could eat nothing but vegetables they called it a “collation” or something like that. I, being a Heathen ate and drank my fill – I went shopping with John afterwards for an

[p. 2]

Easter present for his wife and we bought her a most gorgeous evening wrap for $500. – It was all pale blue and silver and I sure did envy the lady – In the afternoon some fool women, a Mrs. Pierre Mali & others, to see the Library. They made me sick and I threw them out at 4:30 – then home for tea with two gents who shall be nameless (curious???) At 9 o’clock last night I took my headache to bed – It seemed to enjoy itself as it “perspired profusely”
