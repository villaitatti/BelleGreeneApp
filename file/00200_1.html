Monday, July 1, 1912

Dear B. B. –

The matter of “our” Coptic manuscripts is still in “limbo” Morey of Princeton came down to see me several weeks ago to state his competency for doing the work on the illuminations. I

listened to him not only carefully but ever stenographically and did not consider it aside from the question to ask him what work he had already done in that line.
