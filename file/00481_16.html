Now I must be off – I really cannot see that it takes either a discerning or psychological eye to read between the lines of these (from me) uniquely lengthy and practically daily letters.

The weather is vile and adds to – or perhaps causes – my depression – I shall probably run away somewhere soon (I day say to Yama Farms) for a week to 10 days –

Mrs. Wusabella Gardner has sent word that she cannot afford to buy Gimpel’s statue – I have suggested several other names to him – He is wiling & anxious to keep it

Your Belle
