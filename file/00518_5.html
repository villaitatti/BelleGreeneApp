Dear B. B –

I received your letter of the 13th just as I was about to post the enclosed –

As you may not be in or near Florence until the 20th – I will go to the Grand Hotel there and do some sight seeing – Then will do anything you want ( really want) me to for a few days – You

[p. 6]

evidently did not receive a letter I wrote you from Paris saying that I was going to Rome for a week and would come on to Florence then –

In any caseletme know at the Grand Hotel, Florence what – if anything – you want me to do from about the 21 to end of July

[ in print : LOVE]

Belle
