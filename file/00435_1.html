March 31 – 1919 –

Dear B.B. –

You say (in your letter of March 12th which I just received today) that my last letter was the only natural one you had received – I can say quite the same for yours – the earlier ones sounded very much as if you were writing against a strong inclination – a sort of "duty-to-be-done" feeling –
