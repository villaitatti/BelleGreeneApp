I must get to Rome for the Vatican – and I want to ‘do’ some of the hill towns –

I do hope that you are very amusing – and if I find a nice new – brand new – beau, can I bring him along to Florence?

Had a letter from Loeser the other day – and 1 or 2 others of or near your little town – so you need not be swamped with my society unless you want to –

Of course – you know by this time – that I thoroughly approve of your unwithered, but haply transmuted passion – would to God I had the receipt, alter the Heart of me – well
