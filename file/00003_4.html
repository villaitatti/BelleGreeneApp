How I should love to be with you in London – to see those wonderful things with your eyes – I am sure that I could amount to something with you to help me – but who is there here to teach one? – Be sure dear and tell me every thing you do and all the wonderful things you see – and if you see any one of our friends, tell them they are seeing the best of me – in you –
