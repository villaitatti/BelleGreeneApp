Everything upset again –

However I have cabled to J.P. & he can decide what I am to do –

I am really terribly distressed at your accounts of your health – It is too dreadful and I cannot think what got you into this state – I do wish I could be there for several months to nurse you & pull you up – Surely you could get braced up in some
