Friday noon

Darling – this letter is not at all what I wanted to say to you, that is not all I wanted to say to you but for the past two days I have been tied up with Freer who is out here and this afternoon I am going back to Detroit with him to stay until Tuesday – He has over 100 New Chinese paintings!

I will tell you how he got them & write you all about them from Detroit – I miss you so much dear – I just can’t bear to let me mind dwell upon you too long – An Italian duke here tells me that Italy is going to war and he expects to be on the Austrian frontier on Feb. 1 – He looks like a barber & I think he’s a white slaver –

Ever your own Belle
