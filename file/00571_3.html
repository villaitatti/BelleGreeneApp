Have just left Ragusa which I adored – and almost stayed on shore for a few days – Tomorrow we stop at Brioni for an hour – but I am not going ashore as I flitted away a month there once and had enough –

I do not envy you London although I hear the people

[p. 5]

are not so depression-crazed as are we in New York.

I dare say you will see the Duveens in their “lordly” manor house. I understood that the Metropolitan Museum was to bring some of Mackay’s paintings and tapestries at the meeting the day after I sailed, but I have heard nothing since – However the great Joe will dish you all the arty dirt. Thank Heavens I am out of it all for a few weeks at least.

I expect to stay in Venice at the Grant for about 6 days
