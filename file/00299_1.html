Wednesday morning

Dearest B.B. –

You will forgive my breaking the “rule” of daily writing — yesterday. It was the anniversary of Mr. Morgan’s death and I went into the silence for the whole day and could not speak to anyone

[p. 2]

else. It was one of the hardest days I have ever spent and I am still numb and dumb from it – We closed the Library for the day and intensified the hurt of it all by receiving mourners – The great love that so many had for him, was shown by the beautiful flowers
