[p. 8]

Slide, or fall – with-a-thud.

I honestly, truly, hope-I-may-die & X-my-heart, ask only for a soothing oblivion and anonymity.

I would to God my name were anything but "Belle Greene"

Love is not worth a sigh

And I have done with fame,

Dig for me where I lie

A grave Without a name.

Yours – till we scrap again,

Belle
