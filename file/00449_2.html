It has been utterly impossible for me to secure a sitting room here, so we will have to eat in the very badly managed restaurant.

Just received your two letters of Friday and Saturday.

In conjunction with all the other horrible tales I have heard, it makes me fear to go away from here at all – I have already taken accomodation [sic] for Paris on the 4th and am hoping and praying to sail on the Adriatic on the 12th May –

All this we can talk over

when I see you – I am sure you are aching to go sleepsy – bye now –

My room is no. 3 – piano primo

I daresay they will not think it proper if I ask you up – as it is almost entirely occupied by a huge bed –

It is fine to know that you will be here soon –

Belle
