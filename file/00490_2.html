17 June, 1921

Dear B.B. –

I am sending you 4 photographs/ of The New York Historical Society’s “Triumph of Fame” panel –

As I am only allowed to send 1 photograph in a package, you will receive (I hope ) 4 packages/ –

I think they are very good. The first lot did not suit me and I had them done over.

Belle

[ along the top of the page : P.S. – Be sure that Offner does not see these – as he would tell Sherman & the Hist. Socy)]
