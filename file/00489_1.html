13 June, 1921

Dear B. B. –

I do not know where to begin to answer your letters – I have such a nice ( very nice) bunch of them. Think I had better make a business of it and do so by dates – so here goes –

Yours of May 6 – most

[p. 2]

interested in your several encounters with Valentiner and Goldman (whom I do not know and do not wish to know)
