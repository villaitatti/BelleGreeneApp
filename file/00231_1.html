Friday, March 21, 1913

A Glorious Easter to you B. B. dear you [ double underlined: are] a piggie never towrite me! I haven’t heard from you for two weeks now – but I will set you the example of not writing you and not intimating that you are running

after strange ladies! Today is enough to drive me mad it is so beautiful, so gorgeously flooded with real sun, so warm and so balmy. It is a revelation as to what real weather must be. and to us, who have been deluged by rain and gloom for weeks except for a stray day or two
