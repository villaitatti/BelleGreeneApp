We spoke of you several times today – at one time he [^Eugene^] remarked on my different attitude to Read & to you. of course Eugene knows how I love you – he does not know all of course, but he knows a good deal & he is terribly sympathetic. He is such a dear – of course, it would bore me to death to be married to him but as a friend I love him heaps – Now I am so sleepy eyed I must go to bed. Love me heaps & heaps as I do you – Belle

Monday noon

June 1

Dear – Mr. Jack has been in all morning and I am somewhat worn out & discouraged as I have been trying [^to make^] him to either purchase en bloc the Pembroke Library which is going up for sale at Sotheby’s the End of this month or to buy the larger portion of it at auction and so far I have not been able to persuade him to either .
