Freer had bought it for about $8,000 – I was in a rage I can promise you & immediately taxed Freer with it – He got awfully mad at first, to think I would dare call him to account – but the next day he invited me to lunch when he explained at great length that as he had created Chinese Art – that of course he must be given things at half the price others must pay – He was most specious but did not convince me in the slightest – the whole transaction was too brazen – and I frankly told him so –

of course I intended to talk all this over with you – Personally I have publicly announced that I am out of the Chinese market
