Tuesday 6 p.m – 19 April

Spent most of today with Junius Morgan as he is sailing early tomorrow morning – I want to finish this before I go out to dinner and get it off by the same boat. He brought me photos of his place “degli Angeli” I find it most attractive – He talks a good deal of a man named Proctor – do you know him?

Junius is such a dear and I am so devoted to him – I shall be curious to see how you two get along – and shall not be at all surprised to learn that you do not like each or other – I am giving him a letter to deLaborde as they will have the common interest of illuminated manuscripts.
