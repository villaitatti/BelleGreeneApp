Wednesday, May 6

11p.m.

Dearest B.B.

I wish that in some way I could convey back to you – the elation , the inspiration and the confidence a letter from you, such as I received this morning gives me – It is a caress, a tonic and a "rest-cure" all in one, and I was so tired when it came yesterday –

You see darling I depend on you so very much now

for so many things, for I really live more in you and with you than in and with any daily life here And remember you must never be afraid to scold me, for I know you will do so only when I am in the wrong or have taken a wrong and useless attitude and you are one of the few people whose opinion I respect and abide by, so that I shall always be looking to you for counsel and correction.
