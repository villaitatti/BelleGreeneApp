I got awfully tired of Read’s letters, much as I like him and have not written to him now for over ten days. He had planned to spend September and October in France, but I daresay thatis all off now. No more for now – as I must, perforce keep this open until Thursday night – & things will probably come to a crisis before then –

Ever Your Own Belle

Wednesday, Aug. 5

Well, my Beloved – so many things have happened – I have received two letters from you and England has finally declared war – You note the relative importance to me of these two events! How thoroughly I enjoyed your letters. I only wish you had told me more of George Chase – Is he as he says, so fascinating personally that these women come from near & far to have children by him?
