I have been exhausted by the Xmasnoises and festivities in this house for the past few days – Endless young people (young in comparison with my aged self) have arrived and danced & trimmed trees – or rather trimmed a tree various times & turned things upside down generally –

The Family Dinner – 12 strong – nearly killed me – I went to bed immediately afterward with an opiate – and did not recover until afternoon on Sunday –

I have written "thank you" letters until I am (mentally) tongue tied & my right arm paralyzed –

To Hell with the Merry Yule Tide!
