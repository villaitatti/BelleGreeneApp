Well – I’m buzzing in the head now, so I’ll stop for a while – the thing will be settled with Duveen tomorrow and I’ll write you then. One of the hardships I have is Mr. Jack – If I don’t keep him out of the way, he spoils every deal for me – of course you won’t tell Duveen, but he could have accepted 2 ½ millions from Duveen – In fact I really had to lock him up in the room until I told Duveen that it would not be considered – I’ll write more tomorrow afternoon.

I have no thoughts except this business and you. Your own – Belle
