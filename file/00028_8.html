It is so amusing I think he considers that I adore him – which is just as well for there are certain cases in which he is an essential aid to me – but when the day comes that his usefulness to me is absolutely at an end – he will learn a few truths that I think will astonish him –

By all means go and see my adored Boss in London. I think he expects to be there or near there most of July

[p. 16]

I have had such enchanting letters from Mr. Read about their Egyptian trip and Mrs. Hamilton is bubbling over with enthusiasm about it – I am dying to hear all about it from his own dear mouth –

Beloved I cover your face with kisses and I would you were here that I might rest upon your heart – write me every thing about yourself – all that you do and see – I am always greedy for news of you – I may not be able to write for over a week so forgive me in advance –

Your Belle
