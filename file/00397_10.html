Opened this to say that I have just received your letter of the 25 January Like all my English & French mail it was opened by the censor – I will have the photograph of the Cima taken at once & sent to Sherman – we now have the Filippo Lippi in the Library – I [double-underlined: adore] it – Do tell me did you discover it – when & where?

All my love –will write again next week – Please hug yourself [double-underlined: close] for me – I'm quite crazy to be with you. Belle
