Friday to Monday with mother and my latest beau – a man named Baird – I don’t know whether I ever wrote you about him. I’ve seen a great deal of him simply because he happened to fit in with my reform movement – He is very quiet very serious, very calm

[p. 24]

very studious and very much wrapped up in his work – in fact every thing I’m not. He is a corporation lawyer and very solemnly offers me his hand & small fortune (never says any thing about his heart!) once a month. I’m so used to it now that I will really miss it when I give him his real bounce – Mother
