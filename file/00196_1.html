May 17, 1912

Dear B. B. –

I feel like a lark today. The sun is shining for the first time in a week and I am going to motor way out into the country this afternoon

[p. 2]

It is rather annoying to find that mere weather makes such a difference in one’s frame of mind but such is the case with me at least – I had become so depressed by two weeks of continuous rain!

There is nothing of interest here except the political situation
