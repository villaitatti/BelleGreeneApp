Friday, Aug, 28

I find I shall have to send this by a ridiculously slow boat and Heaven alone knows where you will get it – I wonder if you have made any preliminary decision about coming over here – I have so thoroughly convinced myself that it is the only thing for you to do, that I am afraid I will not be able to bear it, if you do not – Surely there must be heaps of work you can do here – I shall be
