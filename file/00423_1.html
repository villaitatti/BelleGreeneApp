Friday Nov 10 –

[written over letterhead: I do not know as yet whether Hughes or Willsoon[sic] is elected]

Dear B.B–

As bad luck would have it, all Englishmen whom I have met, went down to Bakewell with me so I could not get a chance to write – I am scribbling now on the return trip on a dizzily rocking train – and am sure you will be a wonder? if you
