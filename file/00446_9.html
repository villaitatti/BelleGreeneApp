We are going to have a nice cozy, chatty old fashioned exploring party at luncheon (& after) with Louis Clarke tomorrow – Do you know him? He has a nice flat in Berkeley House filled with all sorts or queer & interesting things, Spanish America etc. Congo & from all the out of the way places in the world – and then after luncheon he is going to show us, somewhere, a great secret in the form of a Chinese "figger" Read & Hobson & Raphael are
