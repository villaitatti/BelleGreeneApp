Saturday, June 20

B.B. You Darlint –

After mailing my last letter I suddenly realize I had not enclosed Lauffer’s letter about the jade – I have spent most of the intervening hours searching for it – but where I have so successfully hidden it Gawd alone knows – I will search my effects at home tomorrow – In any case he says the

[p. 2]

carving is of the XVIII century (1718+) – If you feel up to asking Worch about it – tell him that I did not say anything to him as I felt it was your affair – I love it so much that I don’t think I could bear to give it up – and Laufer may be all wrong –

the mails have been horrid and I have no letter from you today – I’m the original Human Pig –
