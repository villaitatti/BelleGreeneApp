Tuesday, July 7

Dearest B. B. –

Please be a darling & forget all the horrid, nasty & beastly letter I wrote you yesterday. I hereby withdraw it all – I guess my little tummy must have been out of order. Today is horrid & rainy, but I am rather glad of it as it has prevented so many people from coming to the Library. I don’t feel much like writing except to say that I love and adore you and could hug you to death Your Belle
