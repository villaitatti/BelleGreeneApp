Tuesday Dec. 27 –

Well daarrling your Christmas letter has had to wait until this minute I have been so busy that I have scarcely had a moment in which to think: There is very little news here which would interest you. I am in the throes of entirely refurnishing my apartment. I dare say I told you that

[p. 2]

I moved last year and took two tiny apartments in the tail end of a very good street but it is the tail end and if one walks one step beyond out house one falls into the slums –
