We reached home about 7 o’clock & dined hurriedly & all of us packed into the smallest victoria I have ever seen & went out to see the Colisseum [sic] by moonlight as Alice’s young man had begged her to do – It really was fine – We got the guard to take us around & he showed us the “piano original” & the various Secret entrances & all points of interest. It was quite wonderful to see those magnificent walls silhouetted against a moonlit sky & I shall never forget it – We also drove to the Forum & St. Peters and I received quite a new impression of the whole place – Then
