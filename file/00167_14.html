I am peeved because I write this – I am not at all – only isn’t a bit the case of the pot calling names? –

My resentment against Ethel Grant is more on account of her fertile imagination that because of any supposed insincerity I have had quite a number

[p. 28]

of letters from her none of which I have answered – The last, received a few days ago was from Carlsbad and was altogether a vulgar, filthy disgusting affair, she writes as though she found the ink for her pen in sewers – But this I presume delights you from her – I wonder how you would like it from me?
