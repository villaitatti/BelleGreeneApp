to Rome – I have written to the Palace, Excelsior & Grand Hotels (as I hear there is not a room to be had in Rome) and will take whichever I hear from first – I must be in London for the Yates Thomspon sale of manuscripts They have not settled the date of that yet but I will hear in a few days – I expect it to be early in April – I am personally much inclined to go by the Fabre line by way of Gibraltar & Naples as I have never been in either spot, but J.P. is opposed to it as he says they are such rotten boats etc. In any case I will write you as soon as I have any definite plans – I hope to stay in Paris about a month –

I am as proud as punch over having J.P. an LLD. of Cambridge – I really do not know any honor I would rather have had for him – except [ underlined twice : perhaps] Oxford –

Write me all you can that will reach here before Fev. 15

Devotedly Yours,

Belle
