B. Alexander’s, where my French professor, “Count” Wierzbicki was giving a Pelléas & Mélisande recital. He read the text and Miss Lillie Lang Collins played – of course you have heard the opera so you know how beautiful the music is. I adore it. The sound of the wind in the trees water rippling along over stones & lapping the edge of the stream the faint call of far away birds the perfume of the flowers & the kiss of the sun – It is transporting.
