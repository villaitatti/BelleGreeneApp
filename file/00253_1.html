Sunday Morning

Dear B. B. –

Now that you are even a little distance away, it is hard to realize that you have been here at all –

It certainly was unreal to see you at the Station and I am sorry now

(in one way) that I did not let Miss Thurston go alone – but still, to see you again, if only for a moment – was not all pain –
