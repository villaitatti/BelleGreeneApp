They gave me a private view of the exhibition of Modern Painting which is to open next week – I think it is splendid. They have some ripping Cezannes, Gauguin, Degas – Courbet, etc Redon etc It is well hung & very carefully chosen – Most of Agnes Meyer’s things are in it as also Mrs Havemeyer – Miss Bliss etc.

I went up to see Kouchaikjis [sic] Antioch Chalice – some hireling was there who had never heard my name (imagine that!) and made so many objections that I dropped my card on the table & walked out – needless to say they have been telephoning ever since – I’ll go up early next week – understand it is a marvel.

Last Friday morning I motored to Princeton with the Dan Platts
