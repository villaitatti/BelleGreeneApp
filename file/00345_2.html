I was asked to be Chairman of one of the Red Cross committees and said I would undertake it provided the money were sent to the English Red Cross – but they indignantly replied that "mercy knew no coste, [sic] creed color etc” so I dropped out – I have no intention whatever of gathering money to take care of German wounded – Leave 'em rot is my nasty motto – I am going to visit the John Ryan’s at Prides Crossing today until Monday Will write again then. Ever Your Belle

Monday Aug.10

You very most Belovedest

I have just received your London letter of July 30-31 – By this time I hope you have decided to come over here – There is just the chance that you may feel that you ought to be in Italy to protect your interests there – otherwise, as an American citizen I should think this would be your natural place – Mr. Jack M. has been doing great work.
