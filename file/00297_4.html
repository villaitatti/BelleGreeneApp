Imagine then seeing (& with you) so wonderful a piece and [quadruple underlined: then] !!! owning it – so you see you are indelibly connected with the loveliest thing I own. I cannot really tell you how I feel about it – You gave it to me so joyously and know, oh! darling that I shall treasure it equally so – Mr. Jack is here & I must run – more tomorrow – I think that you are writing to me now, too. It is so glorious to love you & to be loved by you.
