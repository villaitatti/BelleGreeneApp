a vacation soon

He does not know that I am planning to quit anyhow within five years! I shall speak to Eugene [^Meyer^] on the subject of your finances tonight and report to you tomorrow. I hate the thought of that damned dinner and it sure is for the last time. good bye for a few hours dear – don’t fear that I am going to “let go” and don’t ever think (no matter how I write ) that I am impersonal about you – You are much more with me than you know – Your Belle
