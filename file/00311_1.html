[p. 1]

Wednesday morning – 2 2 3d.

Dearest – No letter yet – because no ship! I forgot to write you last night that I learned Sirén’s ambition yesterday, which is to be the curator of paintings at the Boston Museum – I really think it would be a good thing for them, but I cannot understand why he should want so small a post, except as an opening wedge in America – They are all so strong for him there and he made such a good impression that I should think he stood a very fine chance –

[p. 2]

What do you think? If I am asked (which is not at all likely) shall I boost him or not? I imagine that Wusabella is very keen about him, [ double underlined : but] it may be that she is just trying to tie him up to her & those are not her real feelings – Post told me too that she was strong for Sirén – Later – I enclose the end of a note I got from Sirén in reply to mine of last night apologizing for not waiting for him – Only a Swede could be as clumsy & un convincing as this – I am just leaving the Library to have tea with Gertrude Atherton so more tomorrow – I do love you darling and I’d give a million cents (all I have!) if you were here – B.
