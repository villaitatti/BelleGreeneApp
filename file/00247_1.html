Saturday, Sept. 20, 1913

Dear B. B. –

I have just received your most interesting letter of the 10th from Vienna. My how I envied you! – almost as much as I did yesterday when your cable from my beloved – [ double underlined : adored] Ravenna came – B. B. how could you be there without me! I think I adore it more than any place, (except Verona) that we saw, of course Siena was too wonderful – but Ravenna
