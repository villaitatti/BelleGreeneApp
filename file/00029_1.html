Tuesday, June 29

My dear –

This I fear will be only a scrawl as I am most desperately tired tonight and am leaving town early in the morning – We had an enchanting time at Harvard – The performance

of Joan of Arc was a wonderful spectacle which I shall never forget – As the Sun said the next day she “played before a vast concourse of Bostonians and human beings ” – fully 15000 people.
