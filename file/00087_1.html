Saturday noon –

Dear B. B.

I have just received your letter of Friday afternoon, and I am dreadfully sorry about it all – You surely must understand that it would be quite absurd for me to come to Paris today or tomorrow especially when the Ritz itself is in the turmoil our papers here report. And all the incertitude
