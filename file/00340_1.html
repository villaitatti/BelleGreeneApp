Monday July 27

Dearest B. B. –

Well – I went and – done it – that is, I have been laid up for a week with what the M.D. called “nervous prostration” – Did you ever hear anything so silly? And don’t you think me a goose? I’m still a bit wobbly. I had been working not awfully hard of course, but awfully steadily, in a rush to get these valuations

[p. 2]
