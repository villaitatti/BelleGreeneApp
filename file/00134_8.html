I am not in a letter writing mood as you can see so I’m not going to afflict you with much more of this sad stuff – I have not yet received the further photographs of your paintings I am dying to see them –

Love – Beloved – I love you I could crush you with love were you here. instead

I breathe all sorts of blessings upon your head and pray the dear Gods to bring me to you soon – You are the joy of my Existence here and I’ll hope of my pardon hereafter – [ double underlined : Belle]
