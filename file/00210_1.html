Monday, Sept . 16

Dearest – Even although no good steamer leaves until Wednesday I must write you. Now at once – for I have just read your very dear letter of the 5th from Carlsbad – only eleven days! Isn’t

[p. 2]

that splendid !

I noted very carefully what you had to say about Chalfin and will follow e your instructions and suggestions carefully – I will be friendly but discreet – I’m very glad you put me wise. A great many
