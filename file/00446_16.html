I do not expect to buy more than perhaps one manuscript and a printed book or two at the Yates Thompson sale. Its not a good lot & the St. Cuthbert (which I really wanted) I am keeping away from on account of the B.M.

Well – dear B.B. – You can never say that I do not write you at length. If I had time, I could go on but now I must write some business letters. Just so soon as my plans are settled I will let you know –

I want to put up at the Excelsior if they can give me rooms. I have heard that everything is very expensive in Rome, but I am sure that nothing can be so bad as here

I pay almost 1/3 more at this hotel than I did the last time I was at Claridge's –

My love to you always –

Belle
