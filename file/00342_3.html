if not a financial panic, but I daresay the latter disaster was averted by the closing of the Stock Exchange – Our dear Eugene was largely responsible for that and a great deal of the credit therefore is due him – Mr. Jack M. bitterly opposed him & so did lots of others until they were compelled to see the necessity of it – I sent you an “interview” with Mr. Jack.
