Tuesday March 21

–

Daarrling –

I was very much amused by your letter (the date I do not remember) received yesterday about Miss Grigsby and others of her kind – She has been after me many times since I returned last fall but I have always been very busy – I heard an

amusing thing about her the other day – Jack Cosgrave gave her a luncheon to which he invited only men – Three of my pet men were there – charming boys but to them she would pay no attention, but devoted herself to a man I know named Max Pam – a prominent lawyer here –

She talked to him a great deal about herself and
