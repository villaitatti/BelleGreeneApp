Sept 5. 1919

Dearest B.B.

This is just a line to catch the boat tomorrow.

First to thank you for your letter, which I presume I do not deserve – second to tell you – honest injun – that I can no more keep loving you always than I can keep flirting – always –

[p. 2]

I really want to write you all about it sometime – that is all about why I could never stop loving you. Even if I do not write for years or do not hear from you for years –

But I only have time in this to state it as a fact .
