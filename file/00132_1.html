Friday Feb. 17 –

Daarrling –

I have today received three letters from you after having waited almost ten days for one – They are dated the 29 of Jan., Feb. 4 and Feb. 1 containing all that most interesting information about our doors & the photographs

of the Pisa & Florence doors – I am quite convinced by what you say that our doors are recent forgeries – I am so much obliged for the way in which you told me – I followed it perfectly & was tremendously interested in your method and then youstatedit all so clearly & concisely that I was not puzzled
