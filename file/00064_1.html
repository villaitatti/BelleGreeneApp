Monday, April 11, 1910 –

My dear – I got your two letters, from Rome & Florence Simultaneously, and was, as you may imagine, overjoyed with such richesse. I am so glad that you saw the Boss and that he was so wise, I shall be anxious to hear if you saw him again in Florence. He is such a dear, and although when he is here, I am cross with him every other minute. I

[p. 2]
