Wednesday

Sept. 13 –1916

Dearest B. B. –

First of all do forgive this paper for the present – Well – – – I was pretty well stunned when I got your cablegram – I kept hoping that it was a mistake and that in a day or two a cable would come saying this – but I pretty much gave up hope this morning & so cabled you – I can't imagine what sort of complications could come up at this late date unless Italy is becoming horrid about allowing her population to be depleted – My really maddening
