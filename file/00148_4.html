evidently changed her plans for she has just cabled me that she is sailing for home this Saturday. News which bores me terribly. I was enjoying her absence so much although I do like her – the Blumenthals sailed today. I think you are wrong and that, contrary to being malicious she will prove to be a great friend to me. I have given up all festivities & the most I do is an occasional luncheon or dinner with however only people above your reproach. I have not yet received my painting will you please let me know through whom you sent it – I think I shall cable you about this tomorrow.

All my love to you daarrling and always remember I love you and only you – Your Belle –
