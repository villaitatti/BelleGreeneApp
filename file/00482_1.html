26 April, 1921

Dear B. B. –

Your letter of April 13 from Hyères reached me today only – what a bore these tramp steamers are!

And so you have met Joseph Bédier – and you ask me for 2 pages or more to be – for once – truthful – honest etc. etc . and tell you if his name means anything to me! [ double underlined : Why] should I lie?

Well – it does. and those three words comprise and compress countless pages from me – I have loved his Tristan for more years than I can, at this moment, remember. I have bought and given away at least 50 copies.

For the last few years I have only been able to get [^here,^] a horrid edition on thick yellowish paper
