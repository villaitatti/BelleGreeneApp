Tuesday Feb. 20

Dear B. B. –

My eyes are still disgustingly and & I cannot use them very much – I am afraid you are “disciplining” me as I have not heard from you in ages – nearly a week – I suppose it serves me right but I certainly don’t like my own medicine.

I have been very busy here cleaning out my cellar the “Salon des réfusées” and have been much amused to see the discarded ones again – There is a dearth of news – I am much worried about my Sister who had to return to the Hospital on Sunday.
