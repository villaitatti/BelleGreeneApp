I came home a wreck & tumbled into bed with threats of killing any one who disturbed me which is why I did not receive your wire – you dear – you dear. As I said – “the” Ugolini appeared this morning & dragged me off to the Vatican where he ushered me into the presence of some grand Cardinal

[p. 10]

(whose name I forget) who said that he had been deputed by Merry del Val to have me meet the Pope – on Saturday at 1 o’clock – Well – I was staggered – I did not know what to do at first – not because I was impressed by meeting the Pope but because I hated to appear rude & unappreciative –
