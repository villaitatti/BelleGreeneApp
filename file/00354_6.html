Price Collier’s Germany & the Germans one day last week – He admires the Kaiser enormously, but he certainly does roast the average German – He says that when Germany goes to war, it will be because the Kaiser touches a certain electric button & that the German people will have had no more to do with it than you or I – quite contrary to what these men said today – Thursty is quite furious with me for lunching with Germans and

[p. 12]

for being interested in their point of view, but I must admit it interests me – Their one hatred seems to be for England – whom they think a treacherous snake – they say the French soldiers are brave beyond compare and they swear that no harm would come to Paris if (or rather when) they enter it –

Now I am up to dress you darling – I hate this letter but will try to make the next one better – no letter from you last Thursday –

Your most adoring Belle
