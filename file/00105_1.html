Tuesday Dec. 20.

My dear dear [ double underlined : dear] –

I was so enchanted to receive your long letter of the 5-7 which just reached me today having missed the Mauretania – I do love you so much dear & in such a heavenly way – it is such

[p. 2]

a delicious, such a fragrant love – I think of you with such joy – such joy and belong to you so utterly and completely dear and I love to belong to you – that is the great part of it – You will think me raving to write like this – but I can’t help
