P. G. Grant failed in his business very badly and rather disgracefully last week – He was dropped from the Stock Exchange at once and some very nasty things were said about the condition of his books in the newspapers – One of the Committee told me that the measures adopted against him were very drastic & very unusual – I’m sorry – but expected it for quite a time as the men in the Street had kept me posted.
