[p. 1]

Wednesday March 29

–

Daarrling –

I most assuredly am a poor – very poor – correspondent. but my dear the Spring is in the air & I am restless and cannot bear to sit down to write – Even to you – you Lamb of God – I am still

[p. 2]

taking the rest cure & like it better every day – I did not go out one Evening last week – & did not dissipate at all until Sunday when I went to the répétition Generale at the Opera House of Ariane et Barbe-bleue – I did not care for it at all –
