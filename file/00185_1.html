Tuesday, Feb. 27 –

Dear B. B. –

Will you forgive this awful paper? I have none of my personal note paper left here at the library & the other is so thick.

I was greatly relieved to get your two letters yesterday. It was such a long time since I had heard from you – Over two weeks!
