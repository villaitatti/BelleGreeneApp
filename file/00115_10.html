Don’t think I’m deceitful, for I’m really planning more for you than for myself although you may be inclined not to believe it –

I’m tired to death – went to a dinner, the opera & a party afterwards last night, got home at 2:30 & rose at 7 so as to have a French lesson at eight – have been wildly busy all day – am due at a dinner
