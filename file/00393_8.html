Whenever I see Kennerley he asks about you – and when I told him I have not written you for so long he have me a dreadful call down – I made him very cross and uneasy by telling him that we understood each other so well that it was als not necessary to have the written word – He has lately been made the President of the Anderson Auction Company which is the rival of the American Art Galleries as an auction room – – their affairs were in such a bad state that I do not believe he can show much of a profit for a year or so to come.
