Dearest B.B.

Here we are back in Munich for half a day before entraining for Bremen and the boat.

I had to keep to my bed the day you left and for the day after. It was maddening for my friends had arrived – As soon as the so-called Bronchitis cleared up, I moved to The Europa (at their expense!) and from then on had a grand time, motoring all over the Salzburg region by day and dutifully returning for the music at night.
