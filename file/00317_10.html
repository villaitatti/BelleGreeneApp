Sunday 11 p.m.

I am too tired dear to write – of today's doings except that they passed off [double underlined: beautifully]

Read was wonderful, just tucked me under his arm the minute I arrived and took the whole party in hand. Sat on Agnes definitely twice and smoothed out all our troubles – he also spoke up for you although I'm perfectly willing to admit to you, that I knew it went agin his grain. Goodnight my dearest –
