Monday – Mars 28

Well I had to bring your letter to town with me to finish it and lo! a truly delicious letter from you on my desk – and right on top of an enormous pile – needless to say the others waited while I read yours twice

I hope your daughter-in-law (I’m so glad its “in-law”!) will look me up & I do hope she will come at a time when I shall be comparatively free

[p. 2]
