[p. 1]

Thursday, Aug. 27th

Darling –

The little authentic war news which trickles through our jaundiced newspapers is frequent of German victories and advances everywhere and is most disheartening and oppressive – we have all read with much interest and admiration England’s “White Paper” and approve more strongly than ever, if possible, of her stand – but where, oh where

[p. 2]

are her troops in all this dreadful time? I presume they are being held for “the” battle which may be in progress somewhere even as I write. You must be as sick of “war talk” as I am, but so great is the obsession of this devastating struggle that it is impossible to talk anything else. I spent all day yesterday and fully half of the night working at the Library with Mr. Jack, and did not have a second in which to write you –
