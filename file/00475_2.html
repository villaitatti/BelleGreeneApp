vile figure of Lincoln –

I thought of you constantly on the tiresome return journey – in fact, I have thought of you so much, that I have rather worked through a mixture of feelings about you and now feel [^am^], in a certain way, at a most heavenly peace about you. If you were here and we had a long evening before us – just you and me – you would understand, I am sure.

I am only beginning to realize how infinitely patient you have been with me and what a horrid little beast I am – But, even as I write this, I know I shall probably be the same, again and again –
