Saturday May 16

Dearestest –

After a frightfully busy and hurried morning I am off for a "deserted farmhouse" belonging to my friend Martha Miller at Stonington Conn. Where I hope to do nothing but "loaf and invite my soul"

I think I told you that I was to have a giddy dinner and dance at my house

[p. 2]
