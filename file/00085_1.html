Wednesday7 p. m.

Dear B. B. –

I have just gotten in from one of the busiest days I have had in London & find your telegram & letter of Tuesday which only arrived they tell me an hour ago – I am dreadfully upset about all this strike business.

Everyone here – the

train company,Cooks– the Hotel & my friends all urge me not to try to leave here until train service is resumed as I cannot take my luggage by motor & run the risk of leaving it here or en route & not getting it in time for my boat at Cherbourg the 19th– I really don’t know what to do but as soon as I see my way clear to either coming by motor from Boulogne or by train direct I will
