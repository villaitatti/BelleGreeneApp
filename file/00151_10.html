I have been expecting my photographs every day to send you but they have not yet arrived, I hope you will like them. They are not any good but you know it is difficult to get a good picture of me – I have such a “wriggly” face – Sweetheart I must run now. I will

write you again this week – I love you dear billions & billions and I am hungry for you. I love your letters, they are so dear and I feel dreadfully when you skipped a day – Daarrling no one but you knows how faithful I can be but you know & you will always know – I feel that I could crush you with kisses dear – I hate to have spring come & not be with you – My mouth is on your mouth & my heart on your heart Ever your Belle
