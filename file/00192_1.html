Friday April 26

Dear B. B. –

I’m in a rather perturbed frame of mind as I have just come from a suffrage meeting and I declare I believe I’m won over. At least I immediately sent in my resignation

to the “Anti’s” – I won’t go so far as to march in the “peerade” which they are going to hold, 10,000 strong next Saturday but I won’t either fight them any longer –
