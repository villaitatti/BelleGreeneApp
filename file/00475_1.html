Friday evening

(Midnight!)

Dear B.B. –

I have not had a moment in which to write you, as, on receipt of a rather enticing invitation, I broke from my cell and eloped to Washington, returning this morning. I had only two free half-hours while there, and spent both of those at the Lincoln Memorial – Don't please, forget to tell me how you feel about it – I has simply hypnotized me – and I can could not explain why to anyone else – except perhaps in an hour of halting speech with YOU.

Something happens to me when I stand in, or walk through the columned sides, and have glimpses of the sky or country –
