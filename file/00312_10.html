Sunday 11 am.

Dearest B.B. –

I have reread the foregoing and in the sane and cool sabbath morning, I feel thoroughly ashamed of and disgusted with myself and yet, I shall send it, for I think the reason you want me to write you daily is because you want me , with all my horridness and pettiness and not something revised for your reading – and so I am sending it although I am very much lowered in my own esteem by the pettiness and bad temper of it – this much I will alter, that I will not do or say anything or have an open row with Agoness until I hear from you & have your wise sane and calm counsel.
