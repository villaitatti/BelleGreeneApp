[p. 1]

Thursday, Jan. 16, 1913

Dearest & Most Belovedest –

I would I could “fly” to thee! Truly – I am just aching to see you and be part of you – I have not had a moment’s “let down” as yet. for there are one thousand loose ends to be tied up and I have dictated letters until I

[p. 2]

am hoarse – then I have so many projects on for the Library. I am going to build the bookcases in my room up to the ceiling which means that every book has to be taken out & put away – I am planning a catalogue raisonnée of of the collection of Engravings Rembrandt Etchings etc.
