Tuesday Feb. 6, 1912

[added in upper margin: I meant absolutely what I said about keeping myself free when you are in New York – The real fact is you are all I can stand or want Belle ]

Dear B.B. –

This letter will be very scrawly I fear because I am writing with one eye firmly closed and the other blinking – I have been suffering a good deal from

my eyes lately and only yesterday got up sufficient courage to go to the oculist – he has put vile drops into them which has the effect above described. I am not allowed to use them but if it blinds me I am going to write You today –
