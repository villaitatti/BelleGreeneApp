I am ashamed of not having seen the Colfax lady for some time – I’ve been terribly rushed and busy – maddeningly so, I honestly have had no time to go to any of her teas & things or to ask her to come to me for anything – She is very kindly and most intelligent and except that she wearies me physically, I like her very much – I find her restless, merry-go-roundish, squirrel in the cage, endlessrushing about, quite exhausting – I dare say that she is better at home
