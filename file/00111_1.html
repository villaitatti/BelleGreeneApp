Friday Dec. 30

This is my [ triple underlined : fourth] letter to you this week – insatiable man! I just enclose this [sic] few lines from Marie which I thought would amuse you – I have just written her today to come back to me at once & life will be worth living again –

I am not going to divulge to you any of my sentiments (high & lofty as they are) for you – Don’t forget four (4) in one week – Belle
