[p. 1]

Tuesday, June 17

Dear B. B. –

I know that I deserve a good beating, for the past two weeks it has been physically, mentally and morally impossible for me to write a line –

I foolishly allowed myself to get violently overwrought and excited

[p. 2]

about what! – Housekeeping! – Did you ever hear anything quite so silly?

I awoke to the fact – very suddenly and very tardily that my house was running loose – none of the servants “functioning” and nothing fit to eat or live with, [ double underlined: so] I took it in hand “with a hell of a yell”
