Monday Aug. 3 – the Library

Darling – I am so excited over the war news, I can hardly write, we have reports this morning that the Kronpr. Cecilie, will proceed direct to Bremen, with our 10 millions! and not stop to unload it at Southampton. I presume they will respect our neutrality and at least send it back. A telephone just received from Mr. Morgan’s office says that he is going to hold a big conference to meet McAdoo, the Secretary of the Treasury – I suppose they will devise some scheme of putting coin into the banks – There has however been some talk this morning of closing the smaller banks – but I am sure that is not really & seriously considered – all money has risen frightfully and the rate of exchange is so high that
