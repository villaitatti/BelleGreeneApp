Friday – April 23

Preferé de mon coeur –

I received today your letter from Cintra of the tenth. Is it not disgusting that eleven days should elapse before I have your written words? Your letters have become so necessary to my happiness – strangely necessary indeed and I fear that I am not quite reconciled as yet – I work and play frantically to forget

[p. 2]

you – without avail for you are always there – the background of my days and nights. I suppose that in the course of human nature (my human nature & yours) and if you stay away from me long enough that I will forget you and you will forget me – and that we both will go our separate ways – you the path of fame and glory – I the path of all “sordid victims”

You bid me close prating of my love for thee & instead tell you minutely the sordid happenings
