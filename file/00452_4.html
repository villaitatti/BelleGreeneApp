I loved them for their clean fresh youth, their freshness (in every sense!) their vitality & their sane common sense – I felt – and still feel, as if I were 16 [illegible deletion] and if for nothing else, I would love them for that –

The news today is that the train de luxe is expected to run – so I may be off for Paris & home by the time this reaches you – I am perfectly willing to stay & so I scarcely bother to reply to Warren's pleas for instructions as to packing –

Such a divine – uncanny place this is – I adore it & could be happy here for many months to come – let me know your plans & please thank Mary (& yourself) for my nice visit with you

My love to you from –

Belle
