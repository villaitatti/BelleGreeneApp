Wednesday March 4th

9:30 a.m.

Dear B.B. –

There’s an awful void in my life this morning where your letter should have been – I suppose it’s because I received two yesterday – one late in the afternoon – If I had guessed that I would not receive one this morning, I certainly

would have saved up my treat.
