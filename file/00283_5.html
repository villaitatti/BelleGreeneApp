Later – noon –

I was interrupted in writing by Mr. Jack’s arrival. He is sicker and more like a huge bear cub than ever and has not given me a free moment. I will send this off now, poor as it is, so that it will reach you tomorrow morning. I am going up to lunch with Mrs. Flowerdale as she’s sailing tonight –

My love to you dear, stronger and deeper than ever – Your devoted

Belle
