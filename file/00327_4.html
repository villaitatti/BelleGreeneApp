I presume that will consider us unsportsmanlike. We reached town shortly after seven – each of us going home for a bath and some clean clothes then met at Sherry’s for dinner and after that to the Winter Garden for a “revue” of the shows of 1914. It was pretty poor – worse even than the follies and was too worn out by sitting through it to go to supper at the indefatigable Eugene & Charlie Knoblanch [--]sled.
