It must come – I don’t believe that we either of us could go on, seeing each other so seldom, indefinitely – I shall not marry until you tell me to and I know Mary is fond enough of us both to let us be together for long times if we can manage it

I want to do so many many things together in our all too brief lives – I want to work with you – to travel with you – to read

with you – to hear beautiful music with you – to see beautiful things with you and to be carried in your arms to death with you – I should not wish to live [double underlined: one] moment after you had ceased to live & I will not – Why should I? with you gone all my world is gone and it would be a poor form to try & live – I am quite [double underlined: quite] serious about that – Goodnight – or rather good morning dear my love – I want you to feel encompassed by my love – to feel me whispering in your ear and fainting in your arms – Belle –
