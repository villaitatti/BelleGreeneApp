Saturday 9:30 a.m.

May 9th

Dearest B.B. –

My much dreaded dinner last night for Read, Murray & Oscar Raphael went off beautifully much to my joy & amazement. Raphael is, I imagine of the best type of cultured and well bred Englishman. he is rather goodlooking although not strong enough in type to impress me – he has travelled evidently all his life and has spent months in India,

Persia, China, Japan, Turkey, even Mexico and Alaska and in consequence is tremendously interesting to talk to and broad and sure in his views – easy and quiet & extremely well bred in meeting people. Murray exerted himself to please and entertain and in the opinion of everyone save myself , quite the star of the evening. He talked freely and interestingly of all the pre-Raphaelite crowd whom he had known and made little (and rather good) impromptu pencil sketches of everyone, at the table.
