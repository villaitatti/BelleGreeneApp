Tuesday Oct. 27

10 p.m –

Well me darlint – Here I am at the Library with a Persian gent named Tahizadreb or something that that who is deciphering some of our Persian manuscript for me – He is a small dark critter with the most exquisite manners and as quiet as a mouse – He has done similar work in the British Museum & so there is very little I have to tell him – You ought to hear

[p. 2]

him read some of the verselets around the miniatures. It sounds like the dripping of a fountain and when the poetry becomes “lovery” like the purring of a well fed kitten. He comes every Tuesday evening and we work here until midnight – He is gradually bringing order out of the chaos of those manuscripts for me –
