Tuesday Oct. 21 – [printed text: 191]0

Dear Mrs. B.B. –

I hope you will not think me dreadfully cheeky to write you – I have wanted to do so often since I said au revoir to you in Venice – The moment when all my happiness seemed to desert me – When I got to London I was rather ill & stayed in bed for several days & after that I was so busy with J. P.’s affairs

[p. 2]

that I was not ready to go to London [^Paris^] until Tuesday & lo & behold as I was about to depart the train strike was declared and I was detained in London until the following Sunday – By that time I was too disgusted to do any thing except sit down & wait for this steamer.
