[^& the Hewitts^] go there will no longer be an immediate passage from this villainous, sky rocketing, woolworthy city to a delightful past. I wish I were sure of going with them. –

Sauna Delano (Mr. Walter's neice) & I are going up to Yama Farms on Friday for the week-end – I have a hectic two weeks before me but soon after that will "cut out" practically all evening engagements & settle down to my reading –

You may be incredulous, but I really am longing for that time – I'll be glad to know that you are back at I Tatti – I feel as if I were writing into space –

Belle
