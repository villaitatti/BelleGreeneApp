and would not have been at all surprised if she had been poking fun at me all the time – we are having perfectly beastly weather a drizzling neuralgic and malarial rain all the time. I feel as if I were going to have malaria or typhoid any moment.

I somehow have been thinking so often of Mary lately. Is she quite well and happy? Did she go to Paris with you? If she ever thinks of me please give her my love and tell her I often think of her kindness and generosity and the delightful trips to Detroit & Baltimore I am – quite – mad – to get – to Paris – It seems to be singing in my blood these days. I wonder why? But next year – Beware!

Ever your adoring Belle
