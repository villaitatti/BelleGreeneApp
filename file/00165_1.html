Tuesday Aug. 22

Dear B. B. –

What an angel you are to write me so regularly – If I did not love you for any other reason I would love you for that one – I simply could not exist without you, your love and your letters – and what letters they are – I’m surely going to publish them some day – with plentiful asterisks!!!

[p. 2]

I am so glad you are in your dear St. Moritz & growing healthy and strong How I should love to be there with you – It sure is scandalous the way that Lydig woman follows you around – Designing minx!

I have been simply rushed off my feet since J. P.’s return but thank the Lord he is now off on the Yacht at Newport. He looks very well out I find as you say that he is much deafer than
