Friday Evening

Nov. 17 –

Dearest B.B –

You are behaving disgracefully – I have not heard from you for two whole days – Who is remiss now? I had a simply scrumptious time at Cambridge with my beloved Sydney Cockerell – We went all over the place and saw the most wonderful things and I quite fell in love with Mr. Jenkinson the head of the University Library. He is quite the most alive and

[p. 2]

knowing man hereabouts and with a delicious wit and nimbleness of mind that that simply entranced me. I dare say you know him, but if [ double underlined: not] you must make for him at once the next time you go to Cambridge, you simply must not miss him. Yesterday afternoon I had a real emotional experience (although I am sure S. C. C. ) meant it to be a purely spiritual one!)
