It was curious that Cuyler met his sudden death so soon after they had decided to go out of business. I am glad for Junius’s sake as he was never happy in his office – I shall not at all blame you if you do not like him – but if you do I shall be very happy –

I am having the photographs taken of the paintings you wanted also of the manuscript which interested you & about which I shall be glad to have your opinion –
