[p. 1]

Friday Feb. 7

Daarrling –

This is just a scrawl to tell you I love you – I wrote a 20 page letter to J. P. today for his birthday and I can hardly hold the pen but I don’t want the mail to go tomorrow

[p. 2]

without carrying you a line of my love

The weather here is divine and I am off in a few minutes for Lakewood where I am to spend Sunday

I wish you were to be there to walk with me in the pines.
