(Later)

Sunday pm

Nov 5

Duveen was chortling with his luck for had he remained in Paris 6 hours longer he would have missed his boat & not been able to sail until next week (the 11th) – I spent the week end with the Robert Bensons at their place in Sussex – I suppose you have been there so there is no point in describing it – I love the house – but then all English country homes of

[p. 8]

the “toffs” are marvellous to me – Imagine living in a 15-16 century “castle” They were too delightful to me – took me right into their arms so to speak & I have never fallen victim to a female as I have to Mrs. Benson – She is the most charming, the most wholesome & simple woman I have met here – and what a clean cut intelligence – He of course is always delightful but I never knew her before this visit – their daughter, Lady Wake is one of the most elusively pretty girls I have ever seen
