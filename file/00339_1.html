July 21, 1914

Darrlingest – No letter from you this morning but I also must admit, no steamer. One gets in this afternoon so I’ll surely have one then but I cannot hold this as the Aquitania is sailing at noon – I behaved like a little angel last night – I was invited to the big dinner & dance at Meadowbrook, at the

Piping Rock Club, but I declined & just stayed at home for dinner and read in bed afterwards – “The New Machiavelli” Have you read it? – I think it is wonderful, but perhaps because there is an experience in it almost identical with one of my own – except that mine did not progress so far & I by no means am as clever & attractive as Isabel – otherwise it was
