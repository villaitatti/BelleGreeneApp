27 July, 1936

[ written in top margin: Love to Nicky ( not P.S. Love!)]

Dearest B.B. –

I cannot tell you how wild with jealousy I am that you and Nicky are in that wonderful Ragusa – I spent 2 hours (it seemed to be 2 minutes) there, one time when I was on a

Vulcania cruise, and every year I have promised myself to go back and stay forever – in any event, for the Summer –

I am excited to know that I may see you – if only for a day or two, in Salzburg – that is if I can get rooms anywhere! (my nephew Bobbie is with me) – I took tickets for the last week in August (alas!) as then, I had planned to go on
