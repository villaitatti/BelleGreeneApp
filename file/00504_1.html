31 Jan. 1924 –

Dear B.B –

Your ohsomildandPatient – letter of the 12th made me a bit (only a tiny bit!) repentant of the tirade I sent you – written however, as you doubtless perceived, more to amuse you than for any other reason. I will send you the photographs we have in hand of the MSS. you want and have others taken for you – but after the Brit. Mus. the Bibl. Nat. & the Vatican it's a sorry showing.
