Friday, Nov. 22 , 1912

Dearestest – This time I have been a horrid little beast – but I could give you a line of excuses reaching from me to you – in explanation. But it surely must weary you to hear again and

again of my weariness – I am having to drop out of everything social, for I do not get home until a few minutes before eight and often not until ten – because I often stay after J. P. M. leaves to get off the cables etc, that he invariably thinks of at the last minute.
