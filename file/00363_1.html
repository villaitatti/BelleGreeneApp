Monday Dec. 7 –

Dearest B.B. –

The mails are quite horrid now – the boats leaving once a week or once in ten days only – I have quite loved and devoured your letters from France, that mail reaching us, for some reason, more regularly than the English mail –

I was so villainously interrupted in my last letter that I did not have a chance to tell you, as I wanted to,

[p. 2]

about Fairfax Murray – I may say, without any conceit but with unqualified pleasure, that some of the best and most adroit work I have ever done has been in connection with him. I have not only finished him for Jack Morgan – but think I have achieved the same results with Duveen –

Tuesday Dec. 8 –
