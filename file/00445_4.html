be there. I have made very few set plans for my stay there – Several week end visits, a heap of work at the B.M. – several collections & dealers & the rest of the time is mine.

I suppose it will be largely taken up with men more or less in my own time – Reads – Cockerells et. al. De Ricci is to be there and I doubt very much if I can entirely escape him – we have all decided now that he is, au fond , an innocent but very tactless & somewhat gullible. He wept on my shoulder about his trip to N.Y. this time – He says it has been entirely unsuccessful & he thinks (but is not sure) that Joe Duveen is playing double with him –

He is going to catalogue Mortimer Schiff's catal Italian majolica & I think has one or two other odd jobs –
