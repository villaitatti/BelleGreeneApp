Tuesday Evening Jan. 4

Dear B.B. –

A line in your letter received this morning worries me – "how simple it would all be if I did not care so much for you" –

What do you really mean? I wish I could be certain that my never-absent Egotism were making much of a catch phrase – Somehow I cannot be comfortably sure , and I want you to tell me – (Yes, I know this is incoherent, but that is the way I feel)

With what does your caring for me interfere? How would not caring-at-all simplify your life?

I honestly cannot see that I am anything in it except an ineradicable
