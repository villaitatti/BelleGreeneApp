Sunday 6 p.m –

Dear B. B. – Such a delightful day I have had! I am madly in love with Ellen Terry and when she sends for me I learn all the good looking men on deck & go in to talk

[p. 8]

with her – Nowadays we talk chiefly about Henry Irving & her son “Ted” Craig – It’s too amusing B.B. but she has quite made up her mind that “Ted” & I must meet, fall in love & marry – “But, I said, he doesn’t approve of marriage – he only wants children –” She sighed & said “Yes”– the great trouble is “Ted” will travel & every time he sets foot outside of Florence I have a new grandchild – I ought to be severe” & then she went off into peals of laughter & told me some of his experiences.
