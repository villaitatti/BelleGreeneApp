Freer has just received from China – some of them are finer than the best we saw in Detroit, particularly a Wu-tan-tze-a portrait composition of [^the meeting of^] Confucius and Lao-tze – and a small snowy landscape by Wang-Wei He has two he calls Wu-too-tzen which did not particularly impress me – there is a very wonderful Hsia-kuei landscape which is beyond any description – If the tree in the Boston Museum one, were placed somewhere in Freer's I should feel that it was the most god-like painting I would ever see –
