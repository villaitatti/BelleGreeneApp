Thursday Oct. 8.

Dearest B.B –

I have had such a grubby day – the kind that gets on my nerves because I do not accomplish anything

Murray has been sitting around like a fat and weary tad pole all day –

He is like all the other Englishmen I have talked to, or heard from very complacent about the War and calmly announces that

[p. 2]

England will win – I ask him why he is so sure and replies that England always does win – Pretty poor assurance for them to go to sleep on I should think.
