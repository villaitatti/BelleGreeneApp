Knecht told me that he and Casenove went there, reluctantly, for dinner, and found Hearst’s right hand man Brisbane, at the table – Needless to say, that also was cabled over – I hear that he is now cuddling up to Belgium, Omont asked me about him when here – I simply referred him to any man of standing in the community.

Do please keep all this, under your hat – you may know it from other sources already – No amount of snubbing seems to touch him –

Yours of May 28 – Nothing except the Chanlers – I think she must have been mistaken about Whitney Warren and Swinburne.
