René Gimpel came in for a few minutes. He is sailing today & before I got through with him Sirén was wiped off the slate & he vows to queer him with all the other dealers. He swore not to repeat a word as coming from me, but he seemed terribly grateful for my information – said that before it, he had not been able to understand why Mrs. Kahn who had almost bought the Gentile, so suddenly changed her mind and would have nothing to do with it

I think that Sirén will learn that he was extremely foolish to talk about you in my presence! Goodbye for today dearest – am off to a “moving picture tea” now Your Belle
