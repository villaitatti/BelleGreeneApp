Wednesday afternoon.

I was interrupted in writing you yesterday by a stream of people – among them the Blumenthals who are most tiresomely “devoted” to me at present – due, I think to a little warning to mind their own business which I sent to them via Glaenzer – Mrs. B for some reason unknown to me but which you may fathom was much interested in Mrs. Perkins, whom she thinks knows more about
