Tuesday March 22

Beloved – why do you say in your letter of the 12th. that you had not heard from me for three weeks? I am so sure that I have written you by almost every mail – but as you yourself say “les absentes ont toujours tort”! I suppose I can suffer! – I was not able

[p. 2]
