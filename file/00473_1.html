Wednesday 23 March 1921

Dear B.B. –

In the same way as some men take to strong drink in an attempt to drown a toothache, I have kept myself incessantly busy with people – any one and every one who came along – indiscriminately – in an attempt to banish the insistent thought of you –

At the Library, I have seen a procession of rather bedulling people –
