The night of the day that you left me.

Desire of my Heart –

To settle to what is before me – the hours, the days the weeks and years without you – is to settle to a dull gray despair

or else certain madness – for I can never cease imagining and I doubt not ‘twil be my undoing!

I make vows not to dream of you – and break them! and then “all the doubts that assail thinking men” come over
