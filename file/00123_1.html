Friday 3 p.m –

Just a line dearest before I send this off for tomorrow’s boat – I have spent almost the whole day closeted with J.P. reading over his will to him – He told me I was the only person outside of his lawyer who had seen it so you will hold this confidential won’t you dear?

Iam about wornout now & am going out to get a bit of luncheon – Don’t miss a day inwriting me dearwill you? It is so wonderful to think you have not missed one yet –

Is it wise for me to repeat how much I love you – the telling of it will bore you I fear –

Ever & ever & ever

your Belle .
