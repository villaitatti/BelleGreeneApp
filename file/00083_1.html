Monday 6 p.m.

Dear B.B. –

I was so glad to get your very dear letter of Saturday this morning. You are a nice child after all! I saw Ethel Harrison this morning and she also had had a nice letter from you so you see you have pleased us

all – I am desperately busy trying to get everything finished up by Wednesday. I have ordered tickets etc. for the train leaving here at 2 p.m. Wednesday So I shall be in Paris that night.
