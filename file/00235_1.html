[p. 1]

Tuesday, April 29

Dearest B. B.

Your last letter had a note of tears in it which has upset me a good deal and which has caused me much anxiety about you – I do not know that you were aware of it when you wrote, but I felt it very strongly on reading it. Your health does not seem to improve as it should under the

[p. 2]

wonderful conditions of sun and air and quiet in which you live and I am wondering if perhaps you ought not to get more away from yourself and with people you love and who love you – why not come over here for a while and let me take care of you? I think you need to be loved , B. B. dear, every minute of the day and night – and I am sure my love is not reaching you as I would want it
