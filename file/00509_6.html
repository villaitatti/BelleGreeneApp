Did you get Duveen his new Botticelli and did you expertise the Goldman Fra Angelico as such? And – well I’ll write of some of the many things I have to say to you – and to ask you, in my next letter –

This is really just to pray you have a good year and that all that your heart, mind, soul, and body desires will come to you –

And- if you still really feel that you want to – do write to me –

My love – such as it is – and such as it is worth –

to you –

Belle –
