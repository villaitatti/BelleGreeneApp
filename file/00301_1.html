Saturday evening

April 3 –

Dear B.B. –

If you could perceive me in the flesh – you would perceive a wreck. It is now 10:30 p.m. and I have just reached home – a corpse , so I will not recount any experiences tonight – just write this line so as not to skip a day – I have had a wonderful two days – and enjoyed it all so much – even if now

[p. 2]

my feet and back ache and my eyes are blinded withseeingand (very stupidly) reading on the train –

Goodnight you dearling – Belle.
