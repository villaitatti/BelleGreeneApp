Sunday Evening 8:30 p.m.

You darling B.B. –

I fear this will be a villainously written letter as I am more or less blinded by a headache – one of my old timers which I rarely have now – I awoke with it this morning and have carried it around with me all day, like an old man of the sea – By six o’clock I really could not endure it longer

[p. 2]

so cancelled my dinner engagement and went to bed, where I would be now, were it not that I could not bear to leave you without a letter from me tomorrow.
