I make a great mistake in refusing to be “taken up” by the Smart Set – for I prefer to gang my ain gait– to know the people I like and want to know & ignore the others – I would rather spend a whole evening with my Italian teacher – a most enchanting & learned albeit

[p. 4]

cross, cantankerous and rough creature – than dine with Mrs. Reggie Vanderbilt as I did on the Fourth – I love her place at Sandy Point Farm but when you are dining with sixty people what can you see of the charms of a place or of any one person? – I vow I will never do it again. It is most thoroughly unsatisfactory and a foolish waste of time for me. I
