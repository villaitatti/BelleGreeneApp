[p. 3]

a year or so and see what happens – aussi soit-il

You seem to be overrun by visitors. I am not at all envious of you – my one feeling now is to be “let alone” and not see any more people than I have to. It is quite impossible for me to get away this year – I may be able to arrange for Saturday & Sundays in the country – but I have too much on hand to look for more than that – I feel the responsibility, if anything more keenly now – I shall write you again this week – the men abroad have been too wonderful to me – I could embrace them all – Don’t worry or be puzzled about me – I’ll come out alright Ever your,

Belle
