Thursday 9:30 a.m.

May 14

Huggable One –

I feel as if I had already lived through half a day as I reached the Library this morning at 8:30 and have cleared up all my mail by now.

I spent most of yesterday with the Trio – At Eleven o'clock to the Met. Museum where Raphael and I went carefully through the

[p. 14]

Bishop collection of Jade. I found some very interesting pieces there, several very early ones, among them a Han "badge of rank" of beautiful rusty iron and dull green material very much like my own (by the way our "trio" of experts have pronounced my piece very fine but Sung because of the carving) some very interesting knives and instruments and a "Lo-han" of greenish white jade which is a pendant to one in Lapis lazuli which I hated.
