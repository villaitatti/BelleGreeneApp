Mr. Winship & Bertie Updyke came to call on me & we sat in one corner of a huge drawing room feeling most uncomfortable at the immovable & speechless stare of a semi-circle of dames on the other side of the room. It was hopelessly funny – They looked at my clothes as if they were coming over to feel & appraise them every moment.
