We miss the shock of it all as the whole country, more or less, is on strike so that we are somewhat accustomed to it. I told Miss Thurston what I had written you about Carl Hamilton. She is much interested in him and thinks I ought to tell you that he "strives to learn" – I never noticed it myself, but she has seen much more of him than I, so she ought to know –

I most certainly expect to be here next fall & winter
