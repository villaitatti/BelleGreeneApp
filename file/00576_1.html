3 Sept.

Dear B.B.–

I am so cross to find that I had not posted the enclosed letter to you – and now it is all “no go” because I have had to come over here on business which also takes me back to

N.Y. two d weeks before I had expected –

London is a trem[ switch from blue to black ink : endous ] contrast to Paris, which is deader than any corpse. The people there are all in a bitter mood, which they take out on travellers in endless petty, mean ways, which nearly drive one mad –
