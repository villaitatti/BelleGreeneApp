darling , am I really to have a little bronze? [ triple underlined : Please ] tell me that it is not expensive – Your remember the cost of the wonderful jade – I love & adore it & take it in my hands at [ double underlined : least ] once a day, but I am still a but uneasy about the price – and I ought not to allow you to give me another thing for hundreds of years – So please don’t send me anything except the tiniest thing that costs nothing

All the Same – despite all these high protestations when will the little bronze come? I so hope the man sent it by the grand vitesse – did he?

You must ask him – How I wish I were with you in London – But we would surely be in Claridge’s wouldn’t we?

If I can go over next year, I will spend about a fortnight in London & all the rest in Italy & France.

Your Belle
