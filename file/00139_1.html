Thursday March 16

–

My daarrling love –

Your letter of the 6th received today and telling me of the accident to you has filled me with anguish – daarrling won’t you please be careful – It is pure selfishness on my part for it is because I hate to be made sickeningly unhappy that I don’t want anything to happen to you – I really

grew cold & dizzy when I read that you had been so near a bad accident – Fortunately you were with your dear friends who, I am sure, did everything for you – but please , dear-heart be careful – for my sake –

I am so happy that you had my letters at last & that you liked them. Heaven knows why – I don’t – I dislike writing extremely and I never haveany thing
