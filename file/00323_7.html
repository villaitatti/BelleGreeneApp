After the practice, Mr. Pratt, who was there, asked us over to the Piping Rock Club for

Tea. It is certainly the most beautiful Clubhouse in America, but I never saw it look so wonderful as it did yesterday – The second polo team had been practicing there in the afternoon and so all the pretty girls were out in their most fetching costumes & our English friends, quite lost their heads about the charming little things looking like very gorgeous peacocks
