Friday Feb. 3, 1911 –

Dear my heart – This will be only a scrawl to catch the Oceanic sailing tomorrow – I will write a real letter by the Lusitania sailing Wednesday – I received by the Lusitania today your two letters of the 18 & 22 – the 18th one was supposed to come by the Mauretania Which arrived a week ago – so your

[p. 2]

old Italian post nous a trompé again – How glad I was to get them, even with the lecture contained therein, I immediately set to work to see how many cocktails and cigarettes I could consume in one hour – I won’t tell you the result.
