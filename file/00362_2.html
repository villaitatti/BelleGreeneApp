Tuesday Nov. 3rd –

Darling – after I wrote the above I did go to bed and cried myself to sleep for the first time for many moons – If you could know how much I wanted you and how much I had counted

[p. 4]

upon having you with me this winter! But you can’t because you place such unearthly importance upon mere letter writing – I tell you it’s because you’re all those thousands of miles away that I sometimes cannot bear to write to you – it’s because I want you so much and the minute I take up the paper to write I realize the infernal distance and that simply takes the life out of me – It’s all so different with you.
