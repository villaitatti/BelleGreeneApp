Friday Evening 11 p.m.

You dearling –

I was put “way up in the air” by your train letter which I received this morning.

It sent a rush of love to my heart and brain – and if you had been within arm’s reach you would have been“thoroughly suffocated”

Last evening I dined very quietly and “undressedly” with the Truesdales – She is sweet and fat and feminine – I love him very much and admire him even more. He is so virile, so pulsating with health and all kinds of strength.
