Friday Sept. 25

Well darling, I had a terrible row with Murray last night – My mother had invited him to dinner with some few other people, among whom was Kennerley and a Boston female you have met named Rose Nichols. She knew some female named Stillman who seemed to be a friend of Murray’s & in that way we got around to the fatal subject ART –
