almost drove me frantic Mrs. Knoblauch sent me a little sketch that Gertrude Stein wrote of her (Mrs. Mabel Dodge) but I have not read it yet – I suppose it is in her usual Matissy & Picassonian Style. Write me all that you know of the Dodge lady – By the way don’t hear anything of your Perkins lady. Is she still over here and if so where is she & what is she doing? Lemmeno! I still am suffering from a bad cold – having eliminated Tonsillitis, Grippe & a few other fashionable maladies I now suffer from plain cold-in-the-head – Let me hear from you soon & forget not that I love you more than you (really) know Ever Yourss [sic] Belle
