It seems almost incredible that you should be so low from no cause whatever – of course Love, although I do not speak of it often, you must know that I am dreadfully distressed about your health & your low spirits & your “ retreating ” ideas I don’t want you to be a Trappist, I don’t want you to go to bed when your wife is giving dinner parties & to shut yourself up in your room where people come to see you – Daarrling dear, please don’t do it – please daarrling try to be interested in all the life of your house and when Mary has parties do help her out by being your best & you know how proud we all are of you when you are at your best before our friends.
