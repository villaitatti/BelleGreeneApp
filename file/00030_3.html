I came back to town on Tuesday the 6th– to get (as I supposed) a new supply of clothes & dash off to Alice Ditson’s – but found a cable from the Big Chief saying that he was sailing on the 14 th for home – You

[p. 6]

would be able to understand my consternation if you knew just what this news meant – namely that I was to set the thousand and one wheels of the machinery of “perfect readiness” going – so that perforce I gave up the trip to the White Mountains –
