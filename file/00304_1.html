Saturday 7 p.m –

April 11

Dear B.B –

I am so blinded by this devilish headache that has been my constant companion now for days that I cannot write you as I would tonight besides which I am crushed and furious to have to go out to the theatre at 8:30 – I simply refused to go to dinner but am held to the theatre – so now [sic] more now — I have little to do tomorrow and will write at length then. Ever Your Belle
