I am made perfectly wretched by letters from most of my friends who are in Italy or Egypt or other enchanting places, and having the most beautiful and almost unbelievable experiences –

To think that it is only a year ago since I saw you last – it seems perfectly incredible – the calendars must lie, it is ages, centuries, aeons, many & many an incarnation since your lips touched mine! How

[p. 12]

have I managed to live through it, I wonder? It makes me despise the human frame which persists in going on and on and on – when one’s real life has stopped – is dead –

I wish I were as philosophical as you and could lie content with what we have had – alas! I want more & more & still [double underlined: more]! –

Devotedly yours always

Belle –
