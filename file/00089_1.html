Monday 7 p.m.

Dear B.B.

I have been beating myself ever since noon for having written you such a cross note this morning. It was too horrid of me, do forgive it, if you still have any forgiveness left in your system for poor me – I think I was cross

[p. 2]

simply because I was so disappointed to find you were not coming – I’m afraid the tables are turned now and you are the one who is taking other peoples’ advice for surely you would have come if you had consulted only your own mind&heart
