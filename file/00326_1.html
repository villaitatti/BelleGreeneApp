Wednesday June 10

Dearest B.B. –

We are sweltering in heat which has simply knocked me up. It has descended upon us so suddenly that we don’t know how to meet or, especially in the matter of clothes – I have been wrestling with Mr. Jack all of the morning to buy

[p. 2]

some of the Pembroke books but no success so far – He’s awfully nice but certainly an awful tight wad – I am off now to attend a wedding at Riverdale – so farewell until tomorrow.
