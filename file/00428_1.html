Dearest B.B –

Just a line today au revoir for over a fortnight for you cannot get any steamer letter before then. I have not yet received the letter of which you telegraphed me – Why did you not send it to the [double underlined: steamer]!! as it will doubtless arrive tomorrow Have left strict orders here to have it forwarded

[p. 2]

I have been so spoiled here I hate to go home Had a wonderful luncheon given me today & sat beside the enchanting Col. Repington – the military writer of the Times – they are all so dear to me & I hate. hate. [double underlined: hate] to go – Dear Cockerell come all the way up from Cambridge to say goodbye & brought me all sorts of literary presents – You must tell me all
