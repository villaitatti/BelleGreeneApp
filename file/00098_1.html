Thursday Nov. 3 –

Dear “Angel Lamb” –

I received your most welcome cable this morning but cannot understand how you got my steamer letter so soon – but am very glad you did – I haven’t a minute in which to write &

don’t pretend that this is a letter – but so far I don’t owe you one do I? –
