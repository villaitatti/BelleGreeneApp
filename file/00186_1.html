Friday

Dear B. B. –

This is just a hurried scratch to say I have received your letter in re Mr. Curtis. and that I will write to him, but am too busy to do so today. I will send it by the

[p. 2]

Tuesday steamer

I don’t want the print but will write a properly polite and appreciative letter.
