letter from Murray on Monday – virtually begging me to treat him kindly when he came over – I had a cable from him yesterday, stating that he was sailing on the 12th. I do not believe he will be here more than a week or ten days as there is virtually nothing for him to do –

In case you do come over, have you don’t forget to let me know what to do about rooms for you–

[p. 10]

Now I must close this very unsatisfactory letter & run back to my work –

I wish I could make you feel just how much and how I love you – I think I could be absolutely happy with you all the time, both day & night and I honestly do not know another living soul of whom I could say that – Goodbye you darling – I kiss your blue, blue eyes – and will kiss more than that when you come – Always Your Belle
