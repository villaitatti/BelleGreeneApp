“Crowd”, as you must have found out by this time are terrific bores – and I don’t intend to waste my time being bored – If they had sense enough to put a thousand dollar bill under one’s plate, every time one went to dinner, it would be a decent businesslike arrangement – but I am certainly not going to be worn out just for

[p. 5]

the sake of eating and moving in society.
