Boston May 6th –

Dear Belovedest of Men –

Here I am in “your” Boston and for ten days and without you – an appalling thought – I am making my headquarters at the College Club – another appalling thought. They give me a room about as big as a thimble and have not yet recovered from the shock of my asking for an adjacent bath & sitting room. Needless

[p. 2]

to say I did not get either! This morning I rang to have my breakfast in bed and the manageress came up to tell me as kindly as possible that such were not the habits of decent respectable women such as frequent this club. I meekly agreed and went down to the breakfast room which contained a weird assortment of my sex of all ages – I don’t know why it is that such an aggregation always annoys me – much as I love them individually.
