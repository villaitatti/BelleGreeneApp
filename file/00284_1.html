Tuesday 12 – noon

Dear B.B. –

I am sorry that you did not have my train letter by the time you wrote yesterday and I am cursing myself for having delayed it to write that fullsome postscript at home. It was very stupid of me, not to have mailed the train part at once – Don’t worry that Walters spent his time in my “agreeable” society
