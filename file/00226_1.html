[p. 1]

Tuesday Feb. 11

Dearestest –

I was delighted to receive a nice long letter from you yesterday in which you tell me of your plans for coming to America. If you really mean

[p. 2]

to come over in November then I will definitely postpone any proposed trip abroad – I know perfectly well that it would be awfully difficult for me to get over in any case and if I can see you here, I should infinitely prefer not to undergo all the argument and fuss it would take to get over –
