Friday, July 26, 1912

Dear B. B. –

I am literally snatching a moment in which to get a note off to you by the Olympic tomorrow – I am a wreck! – since J. P.’s return – he has really pestered the life of [sic] out of me – much as I

[p. 2]

adore him and won’t let me out of his sight for a moment – Not a bite of luncheon have I eaten these days – Just simply can’t get away – Fortunately he is going out of town tomorrow to spend Sunday so I can go away too and try to rest up –

I have not dared
