Tuesday Febr 8th

I have had two enchanting letters from you dear man, since I last wrote – The last was from England and was so much more cheerful than your letters from Florence that I was much pleased – It did grieve

[p. 2]

me so to have you so unhappy and uncomfortable in your house in Settignano.
