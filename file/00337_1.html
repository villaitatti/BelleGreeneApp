Saturday July 18

Most Belovedest B. B. –

You will see by the above date, that for the first time, I have missed whole days in writing you, but you are no silly goose and you will forgive me when I tell you that I have not had a single moment free to do so, since my last to you – this infernal work on the art collection

[p. 2]

has kept me here every minute except an hour for lunch & dinner every day and night – It is now six o’clock Saturday afternoon and I am hoping to have at least the miniatures & Chinese Porcelains totaled up for Mr. Jack on Monday morning – but to do that I will have to come back tonight & work all tomorrow – I am telling you all this, not to complain, but to ex plain – I
