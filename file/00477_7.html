By the way, I received a letter from a man named Goulden in Paris (to whom you had kindly suggested my name) concerning his book on Macedonia & Athos.

As I loathe such books in "de luxe" form, "papier du Japon" etc. I wrote him that on your recommendation we would be very glad to subscribe to a copy on ordinary paper and unsigned if such were available. I bother to tell you this as he may consider his signature (at 1200 francs) very important and thus feel himself insulted. Am
