[p. 1]

Wednesday Nov. 15

Dear B.B –

Just a line before I run off to inspect the mss. in the British Museum – I received this morning your letter of Nov. 12 (only 3 days!!) now I want you to sit down & write me in detail all & anything you have heard of me & French & Co – I will write inside at length en route to

[p. 2]

Cambridge tomorrow. I am so full up today – up to a state dinner tonight that I will not have a chance before – Don’t generalize – tell me the names of persons who told you & the names of persons who told them – Joe pretends to adore you & to be very friendly with me, but he watches me like a cat –

I enclose a letter from Martin Egan – one of the partners in J.P.M. & Co.
