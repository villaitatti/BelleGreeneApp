Well Beloved of me, I must stop now. I must write to J. P. by his same mail and I have not much time before six when my massage M.D. comes – I am dining at 7:30 & then to the opera to hear the Secret de Suzanne & the Jongleur –

I know you will not allow such a long time to elapse between drinks again and as I told you I will write at least,
