Thursday, 29 April 1926

[ In upper margin : Am sending the dai Libri photographs by this same post]

Dear B.B. –

You remember I wrote you about my friend Betty Shonnard, who is going to Italy to see for herself (she's from Oshkosh!) if this vague rumour about ART, she’s just heard is true – and

to buy some arty furniture through Diego de Suarez –

She’s arriving about the 10th of May and although I am sure you will scorn her (but she’s so lovable – and I love her), I must have her know you – so won’t you be ‘nice’ – very to her please & relax
