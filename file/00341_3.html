Wednesday evening –

July 29th

Darrling – Please forgive the pencil, I am writing in bed – They make me come home at about 4:30 every afternoon now and go right to bed and have my dinner there – I still feel floppy but am otherwise quite alright Thank you darling for your cable – It was so dear and loving. I go to the Museum unbeknownst to my M.D. for about 2 hours every day – as we have just had word from Murray (after repeated communicating
