Sunday Sept. 12

Dear – This is the first breathing spell I have found in almost two weeks – Affairs have been piling up on each other at an appalling rate and I have been so immersed in my Chief’s doings that my personal feelings & wants have had to be

[p. 2]

brushed aside. He is doing great things these days & I am prouder than ever of him – Thank you for the Cockerell letter which I enclose – It was good of you to do so much for me –
