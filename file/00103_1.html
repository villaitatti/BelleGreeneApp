Tuesday Nov. 22

Dearest “lamb” –

Yesterday I received your delightful letter of the 7 & 8 from Milan: It was so interesting – I love all your letters and never know which kind I like best – When you write me a letter devoted solely to “ You & Me ,” I think

[p. 2]

that must surely be the nicest and then you write me in a fascinating way of all the beautiful things you are seeing & the people you are playing with – like your letter from Milan & I think that surely must be the nicest sort of letter to receive – so you see you please me either way – I sent the grapefruit [you?]
