Tuesday

B. B. dear –

This is just a line as I don’t want to miss that heavenly ship the Mauretania – I have a beastly headache today and don’t feel up to writing but you know I am never to let a week pass again without a line, if only one . I have been

[p. 2]

reading at night too much lately and that has hurt my eyes a bit – Last night after reading for three hours, my eye fell on Brieux’ Les Avariés and I read that through also. It is certainly a powerful plea against this dreadful syphilis –
