Sunday May 3 –

Most Salubrious Biped –

I was ever so much amused by your letter of the 14thApril – Am glad you liked Winship – who can be a G. D. nuisance if you keep him too long – but he is most amusing when he is spitefully and wickedly inclined –

Need I say that it is a ‘help’ to hear that Nicky is not as devastatingly blooming & youthful as before – even so you must be lying when you say that she is aging & fading before your eye
