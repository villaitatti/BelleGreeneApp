Wednesday Jan Dec. 28 –

12:30 p.m.

Daarrling – I have just come in from seeing the divine Sara in La Femme X – I thought I had wept all the tears from my body but oh! love, dear love, your letter of the 18 has made me weep more – It came just as I was leaving the library at 6:15 with a 7 o’clock dinner staring me in the face & so I

put it in my opera bag intending to read it in the motor & during theentre-actesbut the light in the motor did not work & Sara sent for me during the first entre-acte & made me promise to return at the others so it lay under my hand the whole evening unread – Daarrling you did make me weep – weep – no [ double underlined : cry] – To think I made you unhappy by not writing those weeks when I was so wrought up about
