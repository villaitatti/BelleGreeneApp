Saturday Sept. 22

Dearest B. B. –

Not a thing of interest has happened here today and of the war news you are more quickly informed than I –

I lunched with a German !! Think of it! – but I was and am, very anxious to hear the other side – their strongest antagonism seems to be for England and they maintain that Germany to a war is backing the Kaiser

[p. 2]

Monday Sept. 24

I skipped writing yesterday because I spent the whole day in bed, feeling utterly exhausted for no special reason on Earth. My sister and brother-in-law came in the evening for supper & I was aghast to find him strongly pro-German.
