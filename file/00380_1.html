Wednesday, Feb. 3, 1915

1 a.m

Dearest B.B –

Well here I am and such a busy day as I have had all today – I arrived at three with a raging headache, to lay down until five when I had tea with Amy Lowell who has grown elephantine since I last saw her – then to dine with the Crams at 7:30 – I decided not to stay with them as I had an invitation to a dance tonight at the Chilton Club and wanted to go to it.
