Tuesday – June 7 –

My very great dear –

I am afraid, dreadfully afraid that you will think my love for you has changed because I have not written you for several weeks – but it has not abated one iota . I have been in a maelstrom of business, society and emotion for the past month and have

[p. 2]

been so whirled about that I have not had a minute in which to collect my thoughts or even to put the wild & scattered ones to paper –

As in the course of your 40 odd years you have probably been plunged into innumerable such you must forgive me and feel that I am kissing away any hard thoughts that my carelessness may have produced.
