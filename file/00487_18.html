Rockefeller (jr.) refused the trusteeship – when Mr. Ledyard told me (after a meeting) that they had elected him a Trustee, I of course assumed that they had sounded him first – But it seems he has no interest in art – and no time in which to develope [sic] one – Personally I am contente, for I never could see his value to the Museum.
