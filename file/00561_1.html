12 Dec. 1929

Dear B.B.

I hope that this will reach you in time to say Merrie Merrie Christmas – if not, to bring you & Mary all of my wishes for the New Year – It was so nice, after your freezing silence, to have your letter. I wrote you 2 very long ones, long times ago, which maybe you never received, as I never received an answer.

Contrary to your never seeing anyone who knows me, I see people who know you, almost daily, and consequently speak of you – always lovingly and admiringly almost daily. It is indeed curious, to say the least, if you never receive any of my messages.
