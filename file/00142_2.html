I enclose a letter from Miss Thurston which may amuse you – Babbott is a fool – but wise.

She (Thurston) is really worth while even if you don’t think so – I am dreadfully excited over the prospect of having the pre-Duccio Madonna – What a great dear you are

[p. 4]

to me – Indeed it shall go by my bed where I shall lie and look at it for hours – Isn’t it too thrilling that I should have [triple underlined: 2] primitives in my tiny little apartment? My heart thanks you daarrling, endlessly – You are [^have^] probably seen all of my pet friends in Florence by the time this reaches you – Thursty was due there the seventh and J. P. the 8th 1st I believe.
