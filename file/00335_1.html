Friday, July 10

B.B. my Bestest –

I have been almost driven frantic by Mr. Jack for the past two days and worked a good part of the day at the Museum and three evenings here (I am coming back tonight too) about all of which I shall write you fully at my first opportunity, which

[p. 2]

will probably be Sunday – but I could not let another day go by without writing you a line at last – you are the dearest person I know.

Belle

8:30 a.m
