Wednesday Evening,

7:30 pm.

[ In pen : Jan. 12, 1921]

I love you B.B. dear

That is my dominant thought, but is not the reason for this note, which is most heart-brokenly to tell you that I cannot have you and Mary at the Library tomorrow – or perhaps, this week –

J.P. (I hate him!) says that he wants it closed (to all but his personally invited friends) until he goes South next week – the pretext being a desire to be there a good deal, undisturbed, by himself – business conferences – & other meetings & other pretexts!

It is, in reality, the culmination
