Monday Feb. 27

–

Daarrling – It seems to me a very long time since I have written you but it is only three days – There is so little of any real interest to tell you and I don’t dare write you of my silly, giddy doings

I love to think of your

trip with Mr. Curtis & that you are growing very very well and strong It was so clever of you to go away with such a dear friend as he is to you – I shall be anxious to hear all about it –
