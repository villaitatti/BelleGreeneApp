You that lovely jade is to me and how much it will be You all the time that we are separated

It seems rather dreadful to love an inanimate thing as much as I love that jade – but there are so many reasons why I adore it – In the first place it was from You that I first learned of the existence of such material, then You, quite out of the blue, promised to send me a piece – and then I saw Freer’s and loved it
