Wednesday, June 24

Dearest B. B. –

You must have reached Paris by this time and how I envy you – I suppose it is deliciously cool and full of people you know and altogether wildly exciting – I envy you so much that I could scream as I am wild to be there. It is awfully hot here – the steaming kind and I seem to be losing the little flesh I had – I don’t mind losing everything except the price to get to Europe next year – The

whole world here seems horribly dull and everyone of the few people left in it is cross and disagreeable. I am now off to luncheon with a fat English female named Carlisle who is sailing tomorrow – wish I could get out of it – more anon– Your Belle
