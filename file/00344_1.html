Friday Aug. 7, 1914

Dearest B.B –

I suppose it is the same with You and your friends as it is here with us – We are consumed by war news & pay little heed to anything else – My favourite recreation is buying newspapers, but we none of us put any great credence in the reports at the German cable is cut and we get news only indirectly – What a brave showing Belgium is making. Too bad that French troops have not been rushed

[p. 2]

to her aid before this. The anti-German feeling here is intense we all hate ‘em and the populace here takes every opportunity of hissing and jeering. Last night I went out for the first time.
