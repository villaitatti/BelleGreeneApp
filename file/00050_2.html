Your sister wrote me that she is to be in New York for a week shortly – Needless to say I was delighted to hear it & hope I shall be able to see a good deal of her – She is coming to the Library some day –

I have reached a state of bodily & mental fatigue & nervous exhaustion where my one hope of avoiding a total wreckage is to ship the Big Chief to Europe

I really count the days
