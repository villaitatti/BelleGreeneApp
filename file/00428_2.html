I am really quite overwhelmed by the kindness here – I am leaving my sitting room filled (as it has been all the time) with flowers, fruit & candy – I cannot possibly carry it & have sent off heaps to hospitals –

J.P.M. is the only American in good standing here which of course accounts for my reception. I was made fully aware of that today

Goodbye dearest. It is 2 [ double underlined: a.m.] Saturday
