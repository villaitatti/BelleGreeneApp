I have been waiting & waiting for the Bronze – but we are having trouble about it at the Customs House and have not yet received it, so I think I’ll just go ahead & ship the others – I am to see hundreds of people on business & otherwise at the Library today & am going to do the craziest thing imaginable tonight, dine with “Diamond Jim Brady” – I hope no one hears of it – I will write all about it tomorrow – I wish I were able to get over this year, but I am pretty sure now that it will be impossible – but I shall surely come next spring – surely –

With all my love

Ever Your Belle
