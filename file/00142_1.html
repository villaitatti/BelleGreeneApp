Monday April 3 –

Daarrling –

I have just received your letter of March 19 and was as always, overjoyed to have it – While I remember it I want to tell you that I received your photographs and loved them. The standing one was so natural & so

made you as you were when I was with you that it literally made me weep – but I am awfully glad to have them. Also the “Klassikern [sic] der Kunst” came five of them including the Rubens & Van Dyck – I am awfully glad to add them to “our” library and have as yet only looked through them.
