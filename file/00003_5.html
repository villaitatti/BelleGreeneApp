It is really very late dear & I must stop and run – I should like – oh my dear , I should like – Sometimes the thought comes that perhaps it was unwise of me to let the moment slip but then I think of all that I want to be to you and I know I am right to wait – I hope you feel that way – I should be so distressed

I will write again by the St. Paul ^Cecile^

Sailing from here March 23 th rd –
