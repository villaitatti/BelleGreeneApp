Thursday

June 7 –

Dear heart o mine –

I am sending you a “real” photograph such as you demand today – It may look rather sad for

[p. 2]

the simple reason that I was thinking of you all the time it was being taken [underlined multiple times: (honestly)] & that always makes me sad – Even if we meet soon & for a long time I shall never get enough of you to make up for all this hideously long time Belle.
