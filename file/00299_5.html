My only consolation (slight as it is) is that I am certain that I could not be so mad about it, without finding an answering (if dim) spark in you. I will have it photographed if I decide to buy it – no more now – I must run – Will take this to Boston & send it by Saturday’s boat –

Your adoring adoring

Belle
