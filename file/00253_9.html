You see I have gotten into the horrid habit again of gossiping to you – but it is more writing as thoughtlessly as I might talk to you if you were here –

Mr. Sturgis told me what you probably already know, that Mrs. Lydig, who is a sort of cousin of his, is sailing on

the 27th and is going to liveat theGotham – He told me of an awfully amusing conversation with Phil Lydig the other night – which I willretailto you later. He says too, that he had just that morning received a letter from Mrs. Lydig and that her wound is quite healed and that she feels better than she has for many years – You see how unselfish I am B.
