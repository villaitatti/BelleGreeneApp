Thursday, September 17th

B.B. you darling –

I have been downtown helping Mr. Jack in his private office and it has been lots of fun and I was made really quite proud he had a lot of “international” provincial cables & letters to get off & he said it was quite important that they be kept confidential & he could only think of two people he could trust, his secretary & myself – So I was glad – I stayed there until half an

[p. 2]

hour ago – It is now almost seven oclock and I am famished , so I shall go home to dinner & to bed – more tomorrow.

Friday Sept. 18th – noon –

Well darling, the only thing (except war) which has happened so far today is the arrival of Murray – and such a changed Murray – He is amiability and flattery itself – honey oozes from every pore of his body – His boat docked at midnight last night, but he stayed on board –
