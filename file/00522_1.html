Tuesday morning 8 Sept

1925

Dear B.B –

I have been up to Manchester for the Labor Day holidays – and am writing this on the way back.

I had your letter saying that you were going to Germany & Austria the day I left – Friday – and envied you for a long time – I think I was born to be a vagabond – for I continually ache to pack a bag and be off – It doesn’t matter where; so
