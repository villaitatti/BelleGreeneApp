From your cable of the 20th I know you are now in heavenly Paris – the city I love so much – that says so much to me – How I should love to be with you there – what wonderful days we would spend together! To see all the wonderful things by day & to love you & love

[p. 4]

you and love you by night – the thought is intoxicating – I spent last Sunday with Mrs. Laffan at her country place in Lawrence, L. I. – She is still hopelessly heartbroken over her husband’s death and continues to refuse to see every one – I am so glad that I can be of some comfort to her – She has taken a quixotic liking to me and seems to want me with her all the time – It is really quite pitiful.
