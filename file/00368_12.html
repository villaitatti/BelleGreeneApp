Freer was horrified as it seems that Detroit is a “smuggling” point for Canada and although Chinamen are allowed to enter, they are liable to have all their baggage confiscated – so Freer locked the Chink up in his house and sent Stephen down for the trunks – When they were opened, he found that a good many were treasures and after 2 days inspection he offered the man a relatively large sum of money for 12 of them
