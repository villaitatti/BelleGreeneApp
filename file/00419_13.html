[p. 18]

Mrs Benson – an aunt I think –

Benson gave me a copy of his catalogue which you also must have – Your name figures prominently under every picture – The only cool relation I have had was from Cook who sent me a card to Doughty House needless to say I shall certainly not travel all that way to see his collection especially as I have his catalogue.

A man here tells me that Augustus Johns wants to do a portrait of me & that he will write me – I’d love to have it but have not heard from him yet –

Would you care to write up the drawings in the so-called “Giotto” MS. I bought from Voynich & which you saw at Gruel? –

Now I must stop – I haven’t told you all the news but it is the first moment I have had for writing – How I [ double underlined : hate] to go back & how beastly tame it will all be – & I [ triple underlined: hate] being a neutral Devotedly Yours Belle
