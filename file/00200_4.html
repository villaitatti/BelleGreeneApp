I have no intention of deciding hurriedly and so Hyvernat has agreed to wait until I am fully satisfied that it cannot be done here – In any case Hyvernat will be the Editor- in Chief and Chassinat the assistant. I expect to see about six men altogether.

Patriotism has no place in the matter. I despise drooling sentimentality – when arrayed against scholarship – also, I do not intend, if I can help it, that our publication shall be open to criticism –

As soon as my plans take definite shape I shall write you in detail.

Yes, I know some of Mather’s work – I’m on the trail of three XII cent. Italian mss. that are marvels. I hope to land them by the time you get this. Ever yours, Belle Greene
