[ written on left of page :II] Thursday 8 p.m –

Jan. 19th –

Daarrling – Here I am again in bed and again – writing you – I don’t wish to “bray” but I’m doing pretty well I think to give up all dinners & parties & come home to bed two nights in succession – I had two lovely letters from you today – written from Jan. 2-7 –

My letters seem to reach you much quicker than yours do me

The first thing I am going to do is to answer all your questions – I have sent the directions about the grape fruit to Charles & Co – I am appalled to learn they are just reaching you – they of course they will not be fit to eat – They ought to reach you quite regularly now as I send them about every ten days – but if they are not good let me know & I will try Hicks although Charles is accredited with being the best firm here for that –

And I have received the photographs
