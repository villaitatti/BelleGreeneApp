Sunday Oct. 11 –

Daarrling, I was kept so on the jump yesterday that I did not have a minute in which to write – I did not send this by Saturday’s boat as it was a slow one & I knew the Lusitania was sailing on Sunday – I

[p. 12]

lunched yesterday with John Lane who came in on the Lusitania. He talked just the same way about the war as my friend had quoted the London club men – I could have thrown a plate at him – What on Earth is the matter with these Englishmen that they don’t seem to realize their very existence (national) is threatened? Lane has gone mad on the subject of futurism in literature.
