They had been detained for ten days in Frankfort and when they finally were able to leave they were stopped 19 times and twice held as spies – another of my girl friends (a perfect beauty) was stripped to the waist in Germany by a bunch of 2 officers and six men – After they went that far they called in a woman and

I hear from English letters that there are Belgian refugees at Folkstone [sic], boys from 12-15 years, whose hands and feet, have been chopped off by the Germans. and one friend of mine came in today and told of a Belgian peasant woman whose house was entered by the German soldiers.
