Tuesday March 14

Daarrling –

Only yesterday dear, did I get your dear letter of Feb. 26 – I could beat those P.O. people in Italy – It was such a dear letter & so filled with love for me - I had it brought over from the library to my house and

read it in bed where I had been brought from a tiny fainting spell - Don’t be be worried dear – the doctor examined me thoroughly last night & finds there is absolutely nothing the matter with me – I am simply worn out & nervously exhausted and he has insisted upon my staying at home at least four nights a week and that I have promised to do –
