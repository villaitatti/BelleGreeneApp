Thursday April15, 1920

Dear B.B. –

I arrived too late and too exhausted last evening to even write but a telegram to you.

This hotel was and is crowded and all they could [^only^] give me was somebody's salon with absolutely no conveniences –

I was really indecently

dirty from 2 days on the train, but rather than tackle the one public bath, I fell into bed as was.

Now as to my engagements – I know only two or three people here, outside of His Holiness! & the Vatican people – I am making no personal engagements until I see how much time I must give there
