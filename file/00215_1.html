Wednesday Oct. 30

Dearest B. B. –

I was so glad to have your two letters from I Tatti but distressed to learn that you did not find things all settled and comfy for you – It does provoke me sometimes when I realize how I can do all that

[p. 2]

for J. P. and take all such little every day cares etc. from him, that I can do nothing for you – for, strange and unbelievable as it may seem to you I’m really very good at that stunt – making anyone I like very comfortable and fairly happy – which as J. P. told me only yesterday is one reason he won’t let me leave him – oh! B. B. I’m
