Friday March 10

–

Daarrling –

A combination of grippe & a trip on business to Washington prevented me from writing you by Wednesday’s boat. I am alright now & so is the business. First I must write you about the Hoe “Near” Fra Filippo – As I cabled you it was purchased

[p. 2]

by Blakeslee for $1200 odd He sold it at once to one of the Standard Oil men whom we approached through B. – but who refused to sell at any price – Blakesleeboug sold it for $2000. I did all the business through Glaenzer who was so delighted to think he could do something for you that he dashed out in a horrid combination of rain & snow
