Wednesday 12:30 p a .m.

Jan. 18

Daarrling –

The “Varmint” haswent!! We are going to hold a wake over her corpse this evening –

It will be a joyous occasion indeed – I am sure she weighs about 20 pounds less & has

aged 20 years from the strenuous week we have given her but alas! I’m dreadfully afraid we have overdone it. She has told me repeatedly that never has she had such a good time in her life and she is madly in love with Frank Pollock – We
