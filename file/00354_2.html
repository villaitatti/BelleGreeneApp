I was dumbfounded until he told me that it would consist of the editorials, printed in the N.Y. Sun from a week before the war, until the present time – they have been excellent , so I think the book will be quite worthwhile. I will send you a copy as soon as it is out –

Now I am off to the dentist – damn him –
