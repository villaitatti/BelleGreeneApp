War every day – Several Two of my stocks have passed their dividends, a small copper investment and the Harvester Company – the latter due directly to the shutting off of trade in Europe, especially Germany –

As I fear others will follow their example I am curtailing my expenses as far as possible, even to selling my motor – Everyone is doing likewise and I think they are very wise. of course Wall Street is as dead as a door nail, and thousands

[p. 10]

of men, Stocks and bond-sellers, clerks, stenographers etc., are out of employment –

Some of the bigger houses have been able to keep their men on half-pay, and [^but^] a number of houses have closed upbusinessaltogether.

We received a cable from Henry Duveen (in reply to one from Murray) saying that he was sailing on the 28th of this month, which impliys [sic] that he still expects to do business. We shall see –
