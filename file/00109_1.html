Thursday Dec. 29 – 1910

Dear Daarrling –

I am so rich – so very rich for I have two delightful long letters from you – one received last night & one received today written Dec. 12th – You poor Daarrling your letters never come to me

[p. 2]

by the boat you designate

Isn’t it wicked of them! This one was marked Lusitania & should have reached me on the 23d – instead of today the 29th – What devils those Italians are – I love your letters daarrling even if
