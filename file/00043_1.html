Friday – Oct. 15

My first Beloved –

Yours of the 7thfrom Paris has reached me today.

I will answer the questions therein first. – Laffan showed me the your “Pinturicchio” letter the day after I wrote you – I found it interesting but not absolutely convincing – Laffan asked my opinion about exhibiting it – I

opposed the project & will do so – at least for some time. Later on when the talk about it is not so rife perhaps I may speak to in favour of the idea – but I do not think it kind or fair to the Big Chief to make him a buffet for your various factions – and I think you know that my first thought is for him – So that the picture will remain quietly with us when it does come – I
