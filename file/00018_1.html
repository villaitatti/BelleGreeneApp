Wednesday – May 4 –

My dear old nurse – Who has been with me ever since I first opened my eyes upon this fascinating world – came to me in great excitement today with

the enclosed which she found in some shop and which she declares is a speaking likeness of myself at five! It occurred to me that it might amuse you as it did me – I am going to visit some friends who are stopping at Atlantic City – tomorrow to stay until Monday and will write you from there

Belle –
