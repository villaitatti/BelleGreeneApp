Saturday Nov. 4

Dearest B.B. –

I have expected to wire you every day that I was leaving for Paris, but each day I have been told “I cannot tell you until tomorrow about the prospects” –

It quite maddens me to think&that I

am so comparatively near you and can get no nearer. You cannot know from your side how complicated matters are –

From this side I am comparatively O.K – Lord Robert Cecil (through a friend of Alice, Lord Duveen&no thanks to J.P.) would send me over in the military train (only for officers&nurses) [ double underlined : but]
