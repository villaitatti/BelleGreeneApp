[p. 1]

Thursday Feb. 5, 1925

[ In top left corner : Do you ever see Loeser?]

Dearest B.B. –

Really I intended to write you regularly – but – everything here balks me – and now this can only be a line to thank you for your letter – I ‘whooped’ at your description of Rose Nichols, who had implored me to give her a letter to you – but she’s a good old Scout –

I’m hoping to get over early in June – I’ve given up Spain –
