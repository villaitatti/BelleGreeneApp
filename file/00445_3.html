Of course I am pretty sure that all the prices asked of me are for Americans – but I do not know Paris well enough to shop otherwise. This hotel asks me 200 francs a day for my apartment & of course extra for any maid & the meals – Even although J.P. is paying for it, it makes me uncomfortable – I am going to look out for a cheaper one for my return. Someone told me to try the Hotel Vouillemont – do you know that?

My address in London will be the Curzon Hotel on Curzon Street, although Morgan Grenfell, 82 Old Bond Street, will always forward mail & I presume is a bit safer –
