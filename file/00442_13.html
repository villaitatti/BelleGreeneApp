Gimpel has dropped out of the firm here – I expect to see him in Paris as he knows some of the modern painters & sculptures [sic] I want to meet there.

I believe that Duveen has bought a lot of stuff from Austria & that some of the other dealers here are also on that same track –

I don't know what I can tell you about the "Flowerdales" except that George has received the Legion of Honor & Florrie also has some strange & elaborate decoration – It's not Croix de Guerre & that is all I know – They have bought some very fine looking Italians I imagine from Perkins also a curious, Greek looking Italian marble head which I don't much care for, although I may be all wrong indeed – Her house is [underlined twice: too] lovely and I simply adore it – You may not care for it – but for once, I don't care whether you do or not – I simply revel in a thousand and one aspects of it –
