Monday, June 15, 9:30 a.m.

B. B. you [ double underlined: Adorable] Person –

I am “tickled to death” this morning to recieve your letter of May 31 – June 2 – It makes you seem so much nearer to have you say you are going to Paris! I am so glad, both for your sake & mine – for you will have such a splendid & worth while time and will have so much to tell me of – and

then too your letters will reach me so much more quickly! What a meany you are not to tell me what Blyson Burreaughs said about Read – of course he would think him a horrid old dud etc. etc. and I should love to have heard his opinion.
