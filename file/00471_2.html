Monday evening at seven o'clock? My train is due at about five and there will, doubtless, be matters for me to attend to as I have had no mail forwarded etc –

I have been off "camping out" on a mountain top for three days. A perusal

of 3 papers this morning makes one long to run back and stay there forever –

What a loathsome mess have our statesmen (?) made of this world – And that reeking, hydra-head, infested New York – ugh – I shall get out of it as soon as possible after Monday – Will you
