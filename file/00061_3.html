I had a truly adorable cable from the Big Chief today from Rome wishing me a happy Easter, he said also that he was going to Perugia & [double underlined: Florence!!] and after that to Paris, and that he wished I were with him – I did not particularly wish I were with him, but [double underlined: Florence]! – But don’t let’s talk of it or even think of it – it’s too maddening & depressing –

[p. 6]

By the way, one of my little playmates told me today (for the first time) that he knew you well – Rutgers Jewett – he is the head of the John Lane Co. here & published your “Franciscan legend”. I wonder how you like him? We have several mutual friends in common and so I see a lot of him – I think him rather amusing – albeit with a pretty sharp tongue – he is quite a pet of the Goulds which I forgive him because he has other retrieving qualities – By the way do you know a Mrs. Inches of Boston?
