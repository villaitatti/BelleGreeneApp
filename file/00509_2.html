But – even you must admit that I have one saving quality – I always emerge – and in this case I’m willing to wager all I could raise that most women (yes dear – especially at my age!) would have gone under –

That’s why I have not written to you, or to anyone else properly – in fact have done nothing but work my way through the day – and found it overshort for the work – and overlong for me.

[In a larger hand: But now thank God, I’m Free –] and intend to stay so – and I want again to see you and all the people (not many) whom I’ve really always loved in my heart – and they’ve all been so wonderful! There must be some tiny decent thing in me, that they should be so good to me when I have been so selfishly bad to them – and that rather bucks me up for a Vita Nuova –

I wish I had been with you in that Gawamighty Rome – I wish still more that instead of writing me that you had just returned from there – you were writing me that you had just returned from there – you were writing that you planned to go there about April
