Friday morning 9:30 –

I am just leaving for the Museum darlingest and shall send this off at once lest it miss the Saturday boat – It is a vile day – raining cats & dogs – [double underlined: How] I wish you were here – will write again tonight [double underlined: of course]

All my love to you and longing for you – I shall miss you poignantly all today You will be landing tomorrow I suppose – and no letter for yet another week – Woe is me – Ever your devoted
