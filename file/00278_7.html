The afternoon I spent “instructing” Mrs. Jack Morgan and her daughter. It rather tired me a bit as they are rather apt to “oh my” a little too much – Still I am glad to have them take such a keen interest. Then I went over and had tea with them, which bored me a good deal until

[p. 11]
