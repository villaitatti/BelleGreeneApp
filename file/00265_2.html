I take them up – and kiss when I put them back –

Life is so terribly worth while when one has things like these Entre les mains – Junius Morgan came in for an hour and we made up although neither apologized – “your friend” Rose Nichols, sent me as a Christmas card an autographed poem of Ferris Greenslet’s to Ralph Cram – I liked the last verse – “This, I suppose the wise will hold [/] The most important part of you [/] They’re some who like to think upon [/] The eager human heart of you–”
