Paff in conjunction with one Grosvenor Thomas (an ancient Cockney tippler) is showing a most interesting collection of "stained" glass – There is one small panel (the Jesse tree) which I mightily covet – It is of the XIII cent. with the original lead and only sufficiently corroded (?) to give an added quality – Such glorious reds and blues, such splendid figure design and above all a succulent – spring grass green (of in the leaves) that is delicious – It is as if one of our finest manuscripts of the period had suddenly become
