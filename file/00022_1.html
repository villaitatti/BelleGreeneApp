Friday May 28

Minuit

[written diagonally, to the left of the date: O mes désirs! Mes vains désirs!!]

After your harrowing silence any scrap in your handwriting is a joy – therefore I “acknowledge with thanks” receipt of a line from you on the 16th – What do you – mean by the Seristori [sic]? – You say you have written me of her (?) but I have not had

[p. 2]

a word and am jealously impatient to know all –

I have been made faint and dizzy and sick at heart tonight by one of the most sordid plays I have ever witnessed – The Easiest Way– It treats of a world (unfortunately the world of today) in which honesty and decency is non-existent – in which love and self-supporting women are jeered at and vice rules
