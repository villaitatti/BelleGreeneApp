Nothing disagreeable has happened but several delicately stimulating encounters – In any case she is sailing on the first of August – Evelyn Benson writes me that 2 of her devilishly & unnecessarily-good-looking sons are marrying – The eldest to Lady Elcho (dau. of the Duke of Rutland) & the youngest to Lord Dudley’s daughter –

I met theMemysslady when I was staying with the Holfords and thought her altogether charming – Mrs. B. spared me no details and is evidently preening herself – She’s a great little manager & I do not doubt will marry her remaining daughter off to the Prince of Wales or the Duke of York – Dear B.B. – a goodnight kiss to you – Belle

P.S. ( Confidential ) I advise you to keep an eye on Offner and be careful what you say to him – It’s fine of Sachs to give him the fellowship, isn’t it –

B
