Friday Sept. 11

I was so glad to hear from you this morning dear, but distressed to find you still very blue and harassed –

I feel so much the same way and surely you have far greater cause than I. There seems to be some cause for cheer in the news today

[p. 6]

the great coup is evidently about to come off – If only those devils can be utterly exterminated – I am going down to Princeton tonight to attend Sarah Morgan’s wedding tomorrow and shall stay until Sunday evening – I don’t like the idea of being away from N.Y. for so long, but I dare say it is better for me to get out of this atmosphere for a bit – Mr. Morgan & the rest of the family are all going down and it ought to be a very pretty affair.
