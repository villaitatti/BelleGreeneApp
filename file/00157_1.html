Wednesday May 31

Daarrling B. B. –

I had to spend all of yesterday even unto midnight with part or all of my family so did not find a minute to write you by the Lusitania – I feel a very special regard for those two ships because they [illegible deletion] bear our letters to each other

[p. 2]

so quickly – It was one of our horrid holidays, “Decoration Day” and I had promised myself a long quiet day at the Library but the “family” would not have it – instead I was to beg, borrow or steal a large motor and take them out in the country.
