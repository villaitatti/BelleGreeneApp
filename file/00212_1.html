Tuesday Oct. 8

Dearestest B. B. –

It seems ages to me since I have been able to write you, but I daresay you have not noticed the hiatus at all. I am simply rushed to death here at the

[p. 2]

library and my new apartment is such a hopeless mess of furniture and unpacked boxed that I simply give a hopeless gasp when I reach there and plop into bed –

I haven’t either the courage or the strength to tackle it. Never
