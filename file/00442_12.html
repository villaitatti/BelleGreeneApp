I saw Mr. Frick about ten days before he died, at luncheon at the Ritz (he was with Mrs. Ledyard who is one of my dearest friends) – They both came over and spoke to me and he seemed in excellent health – It seems that dear old Roland Knoedler is terribly broken up over his death – I had no idea he would feel it so strongly as I thought Carstairs was much more intimate there.

Glaenzer arrived this week bringing Jacques Seligman's son with him – Florrie Blumenthal is going to have him at a big luncheon tomorrow & asked me to come but I decided I'd rather not – However Glaenzer telephoned and I told him he could bring him to the Library one day next week –

Seligmann himself is arriving some time early in January –
