Thursday – April 29

Bien-aimé – I have your letter of April 17 from Cintra and am so distressed that you had to scold me for not writing – Dear if you knew the mad (and useless!) whirl in which I live you would forgive me and love me still – You know you must love me [double underlined: beyond] your capacity for loving – I am as greedy for your love as I am for your kisses –

[p. 2]

Forgive this pencilled scrawl – but I am writing in bed & cannot find my pen – I wish you were here (my continual mental or written refrain!) – so that I might cuddle up into you – and let you comfort me, for all the blue devils in the cosmos are sticking their poisonous tongues into me tonight –
