It seemed to be a very successful party for the first person left at twelve, Kennerley & Ivinsat one and the Trio at shortly after two –

Saturday night 10 p.m.

You darling – as I was writing the above the Trio came into the Library. They were on their way to see the Altman collection which had been closed but which Robinson had opened for their inspection. They were all quite enthusiastic, to my real surprise about my dinner party and wanted

[p. 5]

to know all about the things I have given them to eat, especially the alligator pears & corn – Read drew me aside and asked me if I would do him a real favour. I said yes without hesitation.
