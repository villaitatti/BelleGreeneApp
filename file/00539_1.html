Dear B.B –

I will write you sometime around 2 a.mtonight tomorrow morning – Doing Volpi’s ball for the Prince & the gondalo [sic] parade tonight just to show the world that I am not alone the stuffy old sightseeing wurrkin’ gal they call me here –

This just a line to say that I rather doubt if Joe Duveen will do your trip as he informed

[p. 2]

me (and the world – in his megaphone voice!) that he has a reservation on every train leaving for Paris from now until Sept. 1, as he is awaiting calls from his various spies en route as to Mellon’s whereabouts and as soon as he hears that Mellon has hopped a train from Paris, he will do the same.
