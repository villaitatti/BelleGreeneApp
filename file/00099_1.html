Friday Nov. 4 – 10 a.m.

Dear dear dear –

I have just received your letter of the 27thby theMauretania and was so delighted to have it. You don’t know how I love to hear from you Fiamma mia – I rush through all the mail until I find one in your hand & then I let every thing– go – even

Wall Street until I have read it – The reason I did not send you a cable immediately upon my arrival in New York was that I was not left alone a single moment – for twenty four hours someone or several people held my hands & would not let me get away – otherwise you should have had it the moment I landed for I thought of you every minute and do still .
