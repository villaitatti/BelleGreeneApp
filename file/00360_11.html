find me in rags when you come as I cannot afford any new clothes.

I think it likely that Steel Common will pass its dividend this quarter (it is selling at 39!) – Hope you have not as much as I – Mother always begs me to give you her love when I write. With all my love (and its a heap) Your own Belle
