Wednesday Feb. 23 – 1916

Dearest B.B. –

Xcuse the paper please. All the rest is so thick & makes such a bulky package I am sure the Censor would not let it through. I received only today your letter of Feb. 3 in which you say you are going to Rome so I dare say my cable has not reached you. I am glad you are going to have a change – I am sure you must need it and your letters have been so adorable & sweet lately that I am afraid you are seriously ill, or else seriously well – I hope you don't recover quickly whichever it is.
