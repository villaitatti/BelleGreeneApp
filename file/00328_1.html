Wednesday, June 17

Dear B. B. –

I have just received, read and loved your letter of June 3–6 – [ double underlined: Isn’t] that wonderfully quick for your stupid old Italian post! Contrary to my usual custom – I will reply to certain parts of it –

To begin with I do not quite understand what you mean by asking what Read & Murray (sounds like

a vaudeville team) have been doing for us – Surely I told you – that they had been paid a certain (and large) sum of money by Jack Morgan to come over and value the collection.
