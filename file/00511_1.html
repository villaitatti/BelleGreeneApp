Feb. 13. 1925

Dearest B.B. and Mary,

I know how overrun you are, especially at this time of the year, by visitors and letters of introduction, and truly , I refuse to write them to you, at least 99 times out of 100 – but, in this

instance I feel that I am giving you a pleasure also, is [sic] asking you to please be very nice to one of my dearest and most beloved friends, Mrs. Lorne McGibbon, whose home is in Montreal, Canada, but who has lived so much in England – and will probably live there in the future, that you doubtless have endless friends or
