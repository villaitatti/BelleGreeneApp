I had a charming letter from Miss Marbury – a result, I suppose of your conversation – although if I were to depend solely upon you, Heaven knows, I would never get alevelledhead –

Marie, unfortunately is leaving tomorrow, to be wed very shortly – I shall miss her as I would a toothache or ahurtyfoot – I have not found a maid to my liking yet – but will start a serious search in a few days – I am frantically busy & frantically tired – My love to you – Ever Your Belle
