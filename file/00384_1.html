Wednesday evening Mar. 3

Darling –

You will, I know forgive this pencil scrawl – I am writing in bed just too exhausted to get up – Much too exhausted to eat anything I received before I left the Library tonight (at 8 o’clock!) your two delicious letters of Feb. 8–13 – imagine – March 3rd!
