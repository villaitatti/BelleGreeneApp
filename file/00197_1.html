[p. 1]

May 21, 1912

Dear B. B. –

Such glorious weather as we are having –

I feel made over and 100 pounds lighter and hungry and thirsty for life and more life –

It makes me pure pagan such gloriously warm & brilliant days

[p. 2]

We suffered for over two weeks from rain, sleet & snow.

I try to run out into the country for two or three hours every day in my little car – generally known as the “Roadlouse” – but it can cover the ground believe me –

I am having a very interesting visit from Dr. Schwenke
