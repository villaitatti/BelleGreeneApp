No more now – I have been writing letters all morning to go by the Lusitania at 4 oclock & my hand is almost paralyzed –

Am having Dr. Genthe to dinner at home tonight and a German lesson later – I don’t have to tell you all that I feel for you, do I. – Ever Your Belle
