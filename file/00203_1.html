Monday July 15

Dear B. B. –

I returned from a long and hot trip to Dayton, Ohio this morning and found your first letter from London – I must admit, it made me feel a bit unnerved to see “Claridges” & then your handwriting

It barely seems possible

that you can be there without me – but at least I can picture you there very often – in my sitting room immediately after my arrival – and again after our dîner à deux and the subsequent restless night that both of us spent.

My oh! my how many things there are to remember. I think that if you like me at all this time, you will like me much
