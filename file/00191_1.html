Tuesday April 23

Dear B. B. –

This is to be truly only a line . I have been and am, up to my ears in work for the Titanic survivors & lost – am on two committees and work until late every night –

of course you too must have been stunned by this dreadful calamity. My heart almost breaks when I think of dear little Harry Widener – Mrs. George is very dangerously ill from the shock –

I will write you a real letter as soon as I am able
