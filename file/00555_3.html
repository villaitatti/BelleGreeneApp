But – if I can possibly side-step it, I will – as I most awfully want to go to Sicily in September. Do you think it will be too hot then? I would take Miss Harrsen with me, and we would just get down on our knees and work –

Did you have a courier there and does one absolutely need one?

I am in a low state financially, and as I would have to pay Miss Harrsen’s expenses, as well as my own, I would rather not pay for a courier, unless it you find it absolutely necessary. but I cannot live much longer without seeing the mosaics, churches etc, etc there
