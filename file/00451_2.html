As I must be in Paris at least one week to wind up my affairs there, and as I must sail for home on May 12th – I am only left about 2 weeks in Rome.

I want to see just as much of you as I possibly can – but you are to decide how much and where.

If you can stand the journey to Rome why not plan to come down there about the 19th and let me go back to Settignano (or I can easily put up at the hotêl in Florence) for Saturday & Sunday the 24 & 25 – I only make this as a suggestion –

I want very much to go to Monte Cassino & Siena – Are they very far – from you – or from Rome? –

Make your plans and I will do everything I can to meet them consistent with the work at the Vatican – Do get well – Bestest
