Sunday Mar. 29 – 5 p.m.

Darling – Only when I have been completely lost to the world in sleep, today, have you been out of my mind. And to my great surprise I have found myself “joying” about you instead of wailing and moaning as I had expected to –

I thought over our parting at the door on Friday night.

I am sure that neither

one of us would have been capable of it three years ago – I think it shows that we have “arrived” and that at last we have such trust and faith and real love in each other – that any parting is only physical and temporary and disturbs but slightly the “oneness” of us –
