Friday, Jan. 15, 1915

Dearest B.B. –

It is such fun to have your letters especially those from I Tatti because they, somehow, seem to contain more of you than do your letters from anywhere else – I am interested to hear that you will write again for Art in America – it will doubtless save the life of that magazine as they are terribly hard put to it, with Valentiner & Bode out of the running – of course we will be delighted to have you write up anything here – Do you want any photographs? In any case, I will send you in about a week a photograph of the ?Saturnino Gatti? Loreto House panel and I’d be tremendously pleased if you would have something to say about this. Unfortunately we must have it transferred
