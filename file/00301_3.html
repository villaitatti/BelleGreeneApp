when gossip about her and her collection was poured into my ears and finally said that I “really did not know her” in order to avoid any answer. I think that all the men in the Museum there (with the exception of that poor old fool Carrington) consider themselves very superior beings; they have a nose-up-in-the-air, exclusive air that rather amused me and fitted so perfectly into the general atmosphere of the place that I did not mind it at all.
