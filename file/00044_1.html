Thursday – Oct. 29

I was so glad to get your letter of the 17thfrom Venice dear, that I thought of sending you a cable, but in the rush of affairs, it, like all my good resolutions – was forgotten. What an allure Venice must have – what a spellbinder – what enchantment – so much do I feel that this must be true, that because I did
