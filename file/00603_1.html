At home – 11 o’clock

Dear Mary – I have just written a disgustingly long postscript to B.B.’s train letter – I don’t remember all I said, but most of it was about Walters – will you read it when you have time? – because I think you will understand, (better than B.B.) what I

[p. 2]

am aiming at in his attitude to W. – He is quite fascinated by you both –

Although we never mentioned the Lady’s name, I left him, with a feeling that the P.J. lady is not doing him much good in the big way –

He seemed rather unhappy and discontented
