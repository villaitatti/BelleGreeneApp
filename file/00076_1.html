[p. 1]

Friday, July 29

Dear my love –

I have a most charming letter from you today dated the 16th! It must have struck a very slow steamer as I have already have [sic] had the one of the 19th – but the more the merrier & happier for me – I also now

[p. 2]

have your enchanting cable saying you will give up all of your time to me in London, Paris & Italy – what a dear you are – I didn’t really want you to do it – it seemed so unfair to you – but I love you a million times more for doing it – You
