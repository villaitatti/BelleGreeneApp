he said that he wanted to fac-simile some of our manuscripts for the books – You know he has now planned to edit these books – series to be called the Harvard classics. I was a bit stunned but promised to go put it through – and he is going to facsimile our mss. of Paradise Lost, part of Faust, Endymion, and several other things. He was much amused to think he had gotten the better of me –

We have a delightful houseparty

[p. 6]

all very congenial (most of the men too young to suit me) and three girls so beautiful as to fill me at once with delight and despair – We shall be very busy this week as there are all sorts of commencement things to do – regattas, baseball Junior prom – senior hop one or two club dances, dinners etc. I go back to town a week from today – Please be sure to mark your letters Personal hereafter so they will be forwarded to me – I kiss you dear – Belle –
