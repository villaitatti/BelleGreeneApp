I am going to tea with Henry Golden Dearth, who is going to show me his Persian leaves.

Worch feels that Freer will surely not buy that Chinese group we saw; and he also says the price is enormous. He is going to telephone me as soon as the pieces come back, so I will let you know what Freer takes.

Forbes (of the Fogg Museum) came in this afternoon. He is going abroad at the end of April.
