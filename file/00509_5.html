I’ve been seeing shoals of interesting people from all over Europe (largely England) this year – and we’ve had – or are going to have all of the art critics – museum directors and sich of the world, before it is ended –

Friedländer got along extremely well with everyone – I liked him too – A poor, wobbly woolly old dear from Buda Pesth [i.e., Budapest] wandered in the other day – one Dr. Therry – How do they get that way in life?

I think less and less of your protégé Offner and he is infuriated at your daring to call our Castagno, an early Botticelli – I am going to have a new Botticelli label made for it! – But B.B. dear – you don’t call that Kann [?] portrait a Botticelli, do you? I simply cannot see it – that is Botticelli in it.
