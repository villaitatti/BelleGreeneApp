Mrs. Astor looked very beautiful and very much like a Chinese doll – she looked more wooden than I had ever seen her – that is her face & body seemed wooden – but Miss Thurston said that she displayed more animation than ever before –

In any case I agree with Mr. Sturgis & the other men who were there – I can’t endure friend Berry and could not bear to have him around me – on the other hand I know, before you write to tell me, that my dislike, no rather distaste has no foundation.
