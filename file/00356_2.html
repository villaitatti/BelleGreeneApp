A letter from George Blumenthal this morning from Geneva [^Bordeaux^], calling the Kaiser & his army most atrocious names – He does not expect to get back here this Winter except perhaps for a fortnight, Florrie wrote me from Nice, where she is doing Red Cross duty. She says that no work of art in the world has ever given

[p. 4]

her so much joy as the sight of a wound which has been properly cleaned and is rapidly healing –

I don’t doubt that this experience may make a woman of her – Seligmann writes me that he has over 40 women in the Palais Logan making garments for the soldiers – It is splendid to find how thoroughly united the French people are –
