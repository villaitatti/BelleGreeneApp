Tuesday – July 6

Just a line, dear to keep my “weekly” promise – as usual, I am dashing for a train in a few minutes –

This living in the country is a good deal of a nuisance as it seems to me I spend all my time running for trains or sitting in the middle

[p. 2]

of a road looking at a broken down motor. I had expected to go to Newport for the Fourth but J.P. asked me to come down to the Library & keep him company & we sat in solitary state until four o’clock when he motored out to our little shanty & stayed to dinner. There
