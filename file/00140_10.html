Now daarrling of my life –

I have written you a very foolish letter – a silly letter – but I am writing as you know just as I would talk to you were I with you at this minute & that is my mood –

I will answer your last letter on Friday – You

[p. 20]

I hope, will have sent me a letter from I Tatti before that – I send all my heart to you daarrling and kiss you & cuddle in your arms & wish that we might be together this wonderful spring Goodbye Fiamma mia – My heart is on your heart & my mouth in yours – Ever [triple underlined: yours] Belle
