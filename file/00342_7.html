Noone [sic] wants to pay his English debts. I am becoming a bit worried about you Don’t you think you would be safer and more care-free over here? Business here is almost at a standstill and men are being turned away from the mines & railroads by the thousands – All the Austrians, Germans & French (men) have been ordered to report to

[p. 11]

their consulates and to be ready to leave for their various armies at a moment’s notice – I'm at least delighted to get rid of some of that muck if only temporarily.
