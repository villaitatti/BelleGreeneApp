Later –

I have just received your letters of Aug. 29 – Sept. 2 and am shocked to have you tell me that your anguish has been such that your hair has turned gray – that surely cannot be possible dear –

Our papers reported over ten days ago that the Russian troops were in Scotland and the advices yesterday confirmed the report of their

being in or near Belgium. We are hoping that this is true in which case it will surely add to the present panicky state of the German army –

I am very glad that you wrote me about the Vanderwelde’s – I will do everything I can for them and think we can accomplish something substantial as you may know a Belgian relief fund had been started in Mr. Morgan’s office & they already have many thousands of dollars –
