As to the war it is monotonously painful – that is, it hurts in a dull continuous way – I have given up all participation in relief work, as I am beginning to feel that surely Belgium ought to be cared for by England and France – Germany, after all is openly her enemy but in the same way England & France are openly her allies, but from all I can glean from the papers, poor little Belgium is being leftsurelyalone to paddle her own cause –
