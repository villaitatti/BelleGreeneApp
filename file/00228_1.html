Friday, February 28, 1913

Dear B. B. –

I’m temporarily laid up with another cold – nothing at all serious, but tiresome and nerve-racking – because, there is an infernal ceaseless buzzing in my

head & I cant hear anything I say which frightens me almost to death – being of a reckless tongue!! – of news there is absolutely none except that your emaciated Mrs. Lydig is going to sell her art objects at auction
