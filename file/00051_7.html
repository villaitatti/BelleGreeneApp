this year – I fear the Big Chief will not get away before March & although I really adore him, he absorbs my strength & vitality to an alarming degree – I had Bode’s pamphlet on his “da Vinci” bust – I must say it looks as if he were alright on the surface – I wish I had time to write more but I have a 7 o’clock dinner & it is now after six & I must get home –

Yours unchangingly

Belle
