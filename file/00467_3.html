It all finished up in his informing me in his most deadly polite manner that the Library was his and not mine, and that he did not recognise my assumption of authority over him concerning it – Bump!

Undoubtedly, I shall arrive there at 9:30 tomorrow morning – but at this writing I am certain (!) that I shall pack up & be off to Florida by noon & let him have his Library to himself until he is jolly well sick of it –
