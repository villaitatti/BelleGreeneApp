Wednesday 8 July,

B.B. you are, at last, a great Big DIVINE Critter –

I’ve had a long powwow with Paul Sachs today & he told me the glorious news, which for the moment of course, he is keeping confidential – I am so thrilled and pleased & happy for & about you that you should have done it so handsomely , so really

[p. 2]

beautifully. Sachs was tremendously impressed by your attitude about it all and he gave me a most solemn promise that he would devote himself to the spirit and meaning of I Tatti (which exists nowhere else) with even more love, enthusiasm & zeal, than he had to the Fogg.

I shan’t see him again before I sail, but he is sending me your letters in a few days now &
