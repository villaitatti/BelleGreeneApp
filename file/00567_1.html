July 31, 1931

Mr. Bernard Berenson

I Tatti

Settignano

Florence

Dear Mr. Berenson:

On behalf of the Board of Trustees of this Library, permit me to thank you most sincerely for the copy of your “Quadri Senza Casa. Trecento E Quattrocento Senese” which you have so kindly sent to this Library, where ti [sic] will be carefully preserved as of great value not only to ourselves, but also to the students of Art who consult the Reference Department here.

Sincerely and gratefully yours,

[ Signed : Belle da Costa Greene]

Belle da Costa Greene

Director.
