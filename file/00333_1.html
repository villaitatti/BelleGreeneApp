Saturday , July 4

Dearest B. B –

Here I am after a most wonderful water trip of six hours, along the Ramapo Hills, through Tuxedo Goshen and the Delaware mountains this place is simply ideal – the woods, the river, the lakes & streams, 100 ft. falls and great towering

[p. 2]

majestic mountains – I have been out doing out of door things until I am quite tuckered out, for as you know, I have not much endurance & climbing one mountain and playing one hole of golf is about all I can do – there is to be a big dance tonight but I shall try to get to bed early – I wish you were here – just you alone –
