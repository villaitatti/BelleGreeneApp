Thursday Sept.

Dear B.B. –

I have just finished reading the proof sheets of your Essays in (not ‘on’ – how splendid!) Method, and to my joy and amazement have found the expectedly ‘heavy stuff’, thrilling and compelling in an almost hypnotic sense – I literally ‘wolfed’ it and grumbled & cursed at every interruption in the

[p. 2]

“swallowing-whole” of it, nevertheless I am left without the slightest bit of indigestion.

It all has (to me) the rhythmic beauty and precision of a great Greek frieze.

I must of course, reread it all carefully, quietly and slowly – fletcherize it, and thus really grasp its every significance, but from this first tearing-through, I could wish that it were made compulsory reading for every student in whatever field of art.
