Belle Greene – The Letters of Belle da Costa Greene and Bernard Berenson

The Letters of
Belle da Costa Greene
and Bernard Berenson

The more than 600 letters exchanged between Belle da Costa Greene and Bernard Berenson comprise one of the twentieth century’s most fascinating sets of correspondence.
Written by the formidable and brilliant Librarian and Director of the Morgan Library to the preeminent art historian of the age, the letters open a window onto the compelling world of rare books, art, and society in New York, 1910–1950.

This website presents high-resolution images and full-text transcriptions of Belle Greene’s 600+ letters to Bernard Berenson. Phase One, amounting to the first fifty letters, is currently available, with the remainder of the correspondence slated to be published online by October 2024. High resolution images for the entire corpus of Greene's letters to Berenson can be accessed through the Bernard and Mary Berenson Papers Finding Aid.
